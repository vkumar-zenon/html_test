Thursday Night Fun Run/Walk
The regular GITC weekly fun run/walk takes place EVERY Thursday night (RAIN OR SHINE) at  7pm - meet on the sidewalk in front of the SSI Casino visitor center) around 6:45 pm as the group takes off promptly at 7pm. Different paces and distances. Plan to eat/drink/be merry with the group afterwards inside of Iguana's Seafood Restaurant in the SSI village. Or just show up there to hang out - around 8pm.

Bring your unwanted old running shoes, tshirts and clothes (all clean please) 
any Thursday night - we'll donate them to the International Seafarer's Center and the Sparrow's Nest, both in downtown Brunswick.
 
Bring your clean unwanted bed linens (sheets, blankets, quilts, comforters) any Thursday night and we'll get them to Operation Bedspread. This group gives beds to kids in Glynn County who don't have a bed to call their own. Operation Bedspread will also pick-up your old mattresses, box springs and bed frames. 

The Golden Isles Track Club (GITC) is located in the beautiful Golden Isles of Georgia - St. Simons Island, Jekyll Island, and Brunswick.  We are a local non-profit organization created to promote fun in fitness.  Dedicated to the idea that exercise can be fun for young and the young at heart alike, walkers, joggers, runners and cyclists all comprise GITC's membership.  We welcome everyone of all levels and speed and invite you to be a member.
