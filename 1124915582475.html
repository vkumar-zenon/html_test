YOUR PRESENCE IS A PRESENT
The holiday season is here, and people often wonder what is the best present they could give their loved ones?

Although there is great joy in receiving a gift you can buy and wrap, the gift of spending quality time and having experiences with your loved ones is priceless. As time goes on, we become inundated with items. The LA Times reported that the average American home has over 300,000 items and there are over 50,000 storage facilities nationwide. On average, families have more clothes and toys than ever before. However, kids typically use only 12 toys a day and extra clothes they only wear on occasions. Many items we buy often get misplaced, donated or clutter our personal space .

Many of us want to give our loved ones gifts that can truly last forever but don't know where to start. Our time is priceless and the feelings we give others through experiences create memories that can last longer than material possessions....
