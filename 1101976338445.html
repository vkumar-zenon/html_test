WE HAVE A NEW GIVING OPPORTUNITY. We are collecting items for "Keep Hartford Warm" this winter. This organization is in need of coats, hats, gloves, new socks & underwear, backpacks, hygiene products, backpacks and reusable bags (these items only). Please contact Keith Rhoden, Jr. for more information.

THANK YOU TO EVERYONE WHO DONATED warm hats, gloves and mittens for HANDS ON HARTFORD, gifts for the children of COVENANT TO CARE, and food for the MANNA FOOD PANTRY. Your generosity is greatly appreciated.   

WE STILL NEED YOUR PLEDGE. Please return your 2020 estimate of giving if you haven't yet done so. Click here for a pledge card. Thank you to everyone who took this step of faith and made a financial commitment for next year. Our gifts will keep on giving as we keep growing in faith!

THE PRAYER CHAIN
Please contact the church office if you need someone to offer prayers for you for a medical procedure or diagnosis, conflict with another person, concern about your job, you grieve the loss of someone you care about, face a difficult decision, battling addiction, or searching for an answer to a question. We are there for you, and prayer works.
Giving Opportunities:

Please consider donating through one of our ongoing opportunities to give:

  • Our Scarf Ministry is in need of warm scarves to hang on the railing in front of the church during the cold winter months. If you knit or crochet (or know someone who does), please consider creating an extra scarf or two. Store-bought scarves are also appreciated. These can be left in the church office.  

  • We collect non-perishable food for the MANNA Food Pantry at Hands on Hartford. Canned corn, beans, canned fruit, cereal, peanut butter, pasta sauce, and canned pasta are needed. You may place donations on the bench in the office hallway.

  • Change for Change: The Change for Change bottle and the spirit of Joan Grant have once again worked their magic as FPC purchased a further 8 goats this week to help families in Bangladesh. This addition to the herd brings our total to 55 goats purchased over the last two years. Thank you to all who have contributed!

  • We collect new underwear & socks and new (or gently used) sheets for children and youth ages 0-17 who are served by Covenant 2 Care. A collection box is in the office hallway.

About Us
This is a welcoming Church, embracing sisters and brothers without regard to age, race, ethnic background, sexual orientation, family status, economic situation or theological viewpoint. We are a member church of PC(USA). 

All church services and activities are open to everyone, members and non-members alike.
Sunday Worship is at 10:30 am. 

We invite you to commit to membership as witness to your faith in Christ, and the mission of Christ as lived out in the life of this congregation. We receive members by affirmation of faith, or by reaffirmation or transfer of membership. Please speak with the Pastor for information about membership.
