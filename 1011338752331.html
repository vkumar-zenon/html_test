Hello!
We had such a good time seeing so many of you in November. Now we're in the heart of our holiday season, looking forward to Holiday Open Studio, and to three great fairs this weekend ! I treasure the heartwarming moments of giving I often witness at events. Once at a fair at Children's Hospital in Boston, a mom and her two kids came to my table. The teenage son was clearly a patient; the mother looked so tired and worried. She said that they lived in Maine, and that over the previous weeks she'd driven back & forth many times to be with her son. They sniffed every single soap and sachet and the mom bought each child their favorite. Later, the son appeared again by himself, wanting to quickly and secretly buy his mother's favorite as a gift. He handed me a fistful of coins and crumpled bills and asked if it was enough. (It wasn't even close, but I told him it was.) I will never forget that boy, now hopefully a healthy young man, who radiated such love and caring. I still remember the way they all smiled at each other, so delighted in their togetherness and a moment of fragrant escape, even in a hospital. May this season bring you many opportunities to share a smile, and to give more love and joy to the world. I hope to see you soon!
Best wishes for a happy and healthy holiday season,
Julie

Julie Scaramella, Owner
