Unity Leaves No One Out

People often ask, "Is Unity a church?" Your idea of what a church is may determine what you think Unity is.  Below are the 5 basic principles that Unity believes in.

1.  There is only One Presence and One Power active as the Universe and in our lives, God the Good.

2.  Our essence is of God:  therefore, we are inherently good.  This God essence was fully expressed in Jesus, the Christ.

3.  We are co-creators with God, creating reality through thoughts held in mind.

4.  Through prayer and meditation, we align our heart-mind with God.  Denials and affirmations are tools we use.

5.  Through thoughts, words and actions, we live the Truth we know.

Gems by Reverend Julie

This Sunday at our Burning Bowl Service, we will release attitudes and behavior patterns that we refuse to take into the year 2020.  That process is reflected in the song "I am free, I am unlimited, there are no chains that bind me.  In that spirit of release, we will create a vacuum in our consciousness.
 
Since the universe abhors a vacuum, next Sunday on January 5th at our White Stone Service, we will fill that vacuum with something infinitely better as we write a letter to our God-self and receive a new name for our white stone. 

The white stone is an ancient symbol.  In Biblical times, when someone had served time in prison, at his release he was given a white stone with a new name on it that represented a change in attitude and consciousness. The Bible has many stories about this. When Abram grew in spiritual stature, his name was changed to Abraham.  His wife Sarai's name was changed to Sarah when she   accepted the idea that in her old age she could have a child.  After his conversion experience, Saul's name was change to Paul and so on.

Your white stone will symbolize your new name or the quality you wish to express in 2020.  When you write a word on your White Stone like loving, peaceful, tolerant, trusting, compassionate, prosperous or so on, the stone will be a reminder of what you are
becoming.

Happy New Year!
May 2020 be a banner year for all of us!
