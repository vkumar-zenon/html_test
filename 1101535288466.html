  THANK YOU FOR SUPPORTING OUR HOPE HOUSE FAMILIES AT CHRISTMAS!

When we started getting ready for our annual holiday party we had just 10 gifts on hand.  After putting out an emergency plea for help for gifts many of you really came through for our kids and we ended up with enough to provide each child with a huge bag full to overflowing with presents.  

We wish you could have seen the looks on the children's faces when they were handed their bags.  Their faces lit up.  The littlest ones were the cutest as they struggled to drag their bags upstairs refusing all help because they didn't want to let go of their bags.  All of this was only possible with the help of friends like you.  On behalf of our families, thank you. 

  

   
HEADED INTO 2020
Dear Friends:  As 2019 winds down we are looking forward to a year of new adventures, good work, finding new ways to reach out to, and serve our families.  

2019 was a tough year in many ways.  The prisons in which we work put new requirements on the way we run our summer camps.  In at least one case it was at the last minute which required us to hire additional staff.  We will need that extra staff again this  year so need to find the resources to pay for these additional expenses.  

We started a new program this year -- our Boost Baskets Program. This is a program that provides a boost of food to our neediest families at the end of each month when food stamps are running out.  In addition, each of the families in the program gets a crock pot and a monthly recipe that is nutritional, delicious and easy to make, along with all of the ingredients to make the recipe.  With the help of a generous donor we were able to buy a freezer to help us store food for this program.  Now we need to get a refrigerator.  This program began with no funding and a prayer that we would find the money we need to keep it going.  So far we have been able to keep it funded but we don't know when that will end.  With massive cuts to the food stamp program we have no doubt the need will grow.  

As you consider your end of the year giving we hope that you will think about Hope House and help us meet the needs that are coming our way in 2020.  

Hope House is a non-profit tax exempt organization.  You can give by sending a check to 6031 3rd Street, NW, Washington, DC 20011; clicking on the Paypal link below; or call Hope House and give us your credit card information and we will run it for you. 

We are so grateful for all of your support in the past and hope you will continue to invest in our families.   

For more information call Carol Fennelly at 202-506-2253.
