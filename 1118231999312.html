Flower Guild Requests Assistance During the Holiday Season
In order to preserve the poinsettias for services through January 5th, assistance is needed to water and remove wilted leaves from them twice a week during the weeks of Monday 12/23 and Monday 12/30. For questions and/or to sign up for any/all of the above activities, please contact Guild chair, Phyllis Wilson at phyllisjwilson@yahoo.com or 203-961-1891 eves. Thank you. 
Flower Arrangers Needed.  The beautiful flowers we all enjoy on Sunday morning are the ministry of helping hands in the church that make this happen. We need extra help at this time to share both the burden and the blessing of this ministry. Anyone willing to help should contact Phyllis Wilson. Saint John's Church will love you for it!
Save the Date: Fr. Wheeler's 40th
The Rev. Dr. James R. Wheeler, 11th Rector of St. John's, Stamford, will celebrate 40 years of ordination to the priesthood at the 10:30 a.m. Sunday service at St. Paul's, Brookfield, CT on Sunday, January 12. He will preach and officiate that morning and St. Paul's will host a reception following in their parish hall. Jim and Carol will host an informal open house for friends and former parishioners at their home of 57 Mountainview Dr., Brookfield, CT following the reception. St. Paul's is located at 174 Wisconier Dr., Brookfield, CT, 06804.
