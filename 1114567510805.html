Featured News
We hope you'll shop, eat and entertain locally in Hermosa Beach with family and friends this holiday season! To help you stay local and enjoy all of the festive events Hermosa Beach has to offer, the City of Hermosa Beach is offering FREE on-street parking in our commercial areas. 

This year's holiday parking program began November 27th and lasts through, December 25th. Free parking will be offered at street meters in our downtown and commercial zones that are wrapped in red festive holiday meter-covers. Please note that the current 2-hour and 3-hour time limits will still be enforced according to posted signage.

Open Enrollment Assistance
Beach Cities residents have access to health insurance coverage through Covered California, a marketplace where individuals and families may get financial assistance to make health coverage more affordable. Covered California Certified Enrollment Counselors are available at Beach Cities Health District to answer questions about eligibility, financial assistance, health plans and enrollment one-on-one, in-person at no cost. Call 310-374-3426 ext. 256 or online.
