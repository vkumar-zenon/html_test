SECOND MILE MINISTRY 

The Second Mile Ministry is touching the lives of people in our Jerusalem. Second Mile Ministry opens their doors every Wednesday to minister to those who come for help. We present God's Word, pray with them, feed them and clothe them.  Your love and partnership with this ministry are noticeable through your prayers, donations of clothing and monetary giving. In 2019, by God's Grace we have helped 2,658 people (1,709 adults and 949 children).  We gave out 1,186 grocery bags of food, in addition to clothes, toiletries, cleaning supplies, school supplies, Bibles, and Christmas presents.  We were able to purchase 52 sleeping bags for the homeless.  We were blessed to witness 8 professions of faith (born again).
