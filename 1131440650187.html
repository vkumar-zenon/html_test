Did You Know...
The Friends of Chabot College has so much to be grateful for this holiday season. This year, with your help, we accomplished the following:

  • Met our goal for raising $30,000 in matching funds for the Hispanic-serving institutions endowment fund.
  • Received over 1,100 scholarship applications from 491 unique applicants. 
  • Awarded over $60,000 in scholarships.
  • Provided $10,000 in emergency aid to students.
  • Provided over $6,700 to local, community-based organizations through our sponsorship efforts.
  • Provided over $3,000 in support of Chabot programs and events such as Earth Week, International Night, Chabot Athletics Hall of Fame, and Flex Day professional development activities for Chabot faculty and classified professionals.

Thank you! Your support made this possible, and we look forward to expanding support to our students and community this upcoming year.

This Giving Tuesday, December 3, 2019, we hope you consider making a donation to help support Chabot College students and programs. Any amount, big or small, can truly help make a difference!

Chabot College FRESH Food and Life Pantry
We have a freezer donation to celebrate! A huge thank you to The Home Depot for donating an commercial-grade freezer to Chabot College's FRESH Food and Life Pantry. A freezer enables the FRESH Food and Life Pantry to distribute perishable food items - in addition to non-perishable food, hygiene, clothing, and other basic necessity items already distributed - to students and the community.

The next step is to furnish the FRESH Pantry with commercial-grade refrigerators and shelving units to meet the needs of Chabot students and the community. If you or someone you know may be able to contribute, please contact: FreshPantry@chabotcollege.edu
