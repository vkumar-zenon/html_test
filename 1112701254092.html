Crystal Connections
 
Great love and many blessings to you! The holidays bring a mix of emotions and experiences. There can be many highs and lows associated with this time of year as we gather with family and friends to celebrate. At the same time, we may also be remembering those who are no longer with us on Earth, but who are in our hearts and thoughts.

Our loved ones in spirit are around us more often than we realize. During the holidays, they are closer to us than ever as they increase their visits to see how we are doing, to check in with friends and family members, and to be a part of our traditions and gatherings. Even on those quiet nights when we have the rare moment or two to ourselves, they hear our thoughts about them and send us their love. They know that being there in spirit is not the same as being here on Earth in a physical form. They know how much you miss them and they want you to know they miss being here with you, too.

The love we share with them is what binds us together in our hearts and spirits. We are never truly separated from those we love. As the holiday season kicks into high gear, our loved ones in spirit usually join in on the excitement and increase the signs they send to us. Keep in mind, the signs will come in a variety of ways using one or all of your senses, such as cravings for foods they used to cook, smelling a scent in the air which reminds you of them, feeling their presence with you, seeing images which remind you of them, or hearing their favorite song playing at an unexpected time.

When you recognize their signs, thank them for their presence. They work hard to help you recognize when they are around and love it when you acknowledge them. If you are having a little difficulty recognizing their signs, please know there are no coincidences. Unusual occurrences can usually be traced back to our loved ones in spirit trying to get our attention. Be kind to yourself and know they are there with you whenever you need them.

One of the main reasons I teach is because I love to help people learn how to connect with their loved ones in spirit. Once you learn how they communicate with us across dimensions, you can more easily recognize their signs and interpret their messages to you. They love to help us with our lives, and I love helping you to receive that help in a direct way. In January, I am teaching my Communicate with Spirit Workshop for Beginners. It is a great workshop for learning about how your loved ones in spirit communicate. Other workshops coming up include my Reiki 1 Workshop for learning how to apply Reiki energy healing to yourself and others, as well as my Tarot for Beginners, in which I teach you methods for receiving messages from your loved ones in spirit and the angels using the cards. Click here to learn more about my upcoming workshops.

There are times in our lives when we are unsure about where our paths will lead us or where we are meant to be. To help you on your quest, I have revived my Inspirational Blog article, " Defining Your Life Purpose." It is a helpful guide to assist you with the discovery of what you are meant to be doing in this lifetime. Click here to read more about how you can discover your life purpose.

For our Crystal Connection this month, my guys and gals in spirit indicated we need Zincite. It is a great crystal for reenergizing and creativity - just what we need at this time of year. To learn more about this powerful stone, click here.

I am sending much love to you and all your loved ones on Earth and in Heaven. May you experience the love of your loved ones in spirit and the angels this holiday season.

Defining Your Life Purpose

For some, the recognition of a life purpose comes as a calling or a moment of complete understanding of what your mission is in this lifetime. It is a very strong inner-knowing of your purpose that cannot be denied. For me, it was an amazing moment in which I finally understood what my calling was in life. The events leading up to this moment were carefully orchestrated by my guys and gals in heaven, also known as my heavenly support team. As the realization hit me that I was a spirit medium, I experienced a vision in which every experience in my life lit up like a star in a sapphire blue sky. The stars lit up one by one in a spiral curving inward which was signifying my earthly journey from one level to the next. The center star shined the brightest and magnified in intensity as I looked at it. I knew in my soul that the center star represented that exact moment in time in which I finally realized I was a spirit medium. I knew it was my calling to help people through the wondrous gifts I had been given. It truly was one of the most profound moments of my life.

Zincite - The Crystal of Reenergizing and Creativity

Zincite helps to replenish depleted energy - perfect for the holiday season - and inspires creativity in all areas of your life. It helps spur you into action to help manifest your dreams, release old blocks, and to see clearly which steps to take. It attracts abundance on the physical and spiritual levels and helps open your mind to possibilities. Zincite is a powerful crystal with strong energies tied to Archangel Metatron, who is responsible for helping to instigate positive changes in your life with clarity and renewed energy.

This uplifting crystal is a wonderful stone to carry or to place within your environment. If you are using it often, it helps to cleanse residual energies and recharge it by smudging it with sage or allowing it to sit in moonlight. It originates from the United States, Italy, and Poland and comes in varieties of brilliant reds, yellows, oranges, greens, and clear. Zincite can be located in specialty rock shops or online.

If you do not own this crystal, look at the photograph of the crystal and meditate on its properties to help bring its energies into your life.
