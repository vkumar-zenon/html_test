NEW LIVE CLASSES STARTED IN SEPTEMBER...

Day 1: Dementia- = 6 CEUs
 
Wednesday: 8 AM-3PM
The 7 Stages: This course will focus  on the stages of cognitive decline beginning with Mild cognitive  Impairment to severe late stage dementia.  The Stages of Dementia are  progressive and each individual progress through these stages in  different way. Knowing these stages helps healthcare providers make  decisions about how to care for someone who has Alzheimer disease.
 
Dementia  Behaviors: This two-hour course will focus on understanding and  responding to dementia related behaviors. This course is designed to  provide practical information and resources to help those caring for  individuals with dementia. In this course we will learn to decipher  behaviors, learn the not so obvious reasons for some behaviors and  determine how best to respond.

*this course is approved for RCFE & ARF only
 
Day 2: Sexual Harassment and  Abusive Conduct Prevention:- = 4 CEUs

Thursday: 8AM-12PM
 Aside from it being the law in California,  If you allow sexual harassment to flourish in your workplace, you will  pay a high price in poor employee morale, low productivity, and  lawsuits. This course will cover what sexual harassment, bullying and  workplace violence is, who is liable under the law, how to conduct a  review in an effort to avoid the many costs of this unwanted behavior  & create a healthy, safe work environment for all.
RCFE, ARF, GH, STRTP Approved.

Question:
Will my administrators earn ceus with this program?

Yes, we have over 30 hours/ceus for your administrators to choose from. The Ceus have CDSS approval. 

Question: Do you have an Initial Staff Training Program?
Answer: Yes, We have an initial staff training program as well as an annual staff training program. Just add your initial 16 hrs o f required hands-on training/shadowing to it and you will be in compliance.

Question: What if I am a six bed and don't have 6 people to train?
Answer: We are happy to accommodate 6 bed facilities. We have a flat rate of $50 a month and you may train as few as 1 or as many as 5 people. Rate increases by $7 for each additional person after 5.

Question: Is there a costly start up fee?
Answer: No! We do not charge a start up fee. Just give us a call and we can get you started today!

Question: I work nights. Can I complete my training any time?
Answer: Yes, once you have been put in the training system, you can login day or night.

Question: What if I get interrupted before I complete a course?
Answer: Rest at ease. Our system saves your progress. Just close the class, wait for the message that says "data saved" and you are free to pick it up where you left off at a later date.
