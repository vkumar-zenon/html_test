High School Student Tutors
Beginning November 18, high school student tutors will be available to work with students at CMS. Monday, Tuesday and Thursday after school, EPHS students will be in the Success Center (room 107) to work with students on any school subject requested. To sign up for a tutor, CMS students will report to room 107 after school and request a tutor during sign-in. When the high school students arrive, they are assigned to and assist students from approximately 2:45 to 3:30 pm. There is no charge for this tutoring service. If you have questions regarding the EPHS student tutors please contact your student's counselor.
e-Learning in Eden Prairie
In the event of multiple school closures due to inclement weather, we will implement e-Learning days at CMS in accordance with MN Statute, section 120A.414. An "e-Learning day" will feature an online learning experience for students where assignments will be provided through Schoology and teachers will be available for direct communication via Schoology, email, or phone. Attendance will be taken on e-Learning days based on student engagement and assignment submission in Schoology.

Here are some details surrounding an e-Learning day:
  • Assignments will be posted for students by 9:30am.
  • Attendance will be taken and recorded by 3:30pm.
  • Teachers will be available for communication from 9:30am-3:30pm.
  • Families will be notified of an e-Learning day at least two hours prior to the regular start time.
  • Free resources are available through the district for any household that does not have internet access.
