We are pleased to introduce our new Administrative Assistant,  Lisa Huberty! Lisa will be the one to greet you when you come through the front door and call the church office on the phone.  

Lisa, lives locally and who moved to Santa Cruz just a short time ago. Lisa says that she loves the small town feeling, the beach and the beautiful views that Santa Cruz has to offer. Lisa brings with her a wealth of knowledge and a great desire to learn more about our faith community. 

Make sure to stop by after the new year to welcome her to our beloved community!

Helping Hands
Help our Association of Faith Communities (AFC), Shelter Program this Christmas season. As a follow up to National Toilet Day, Peace's World and Community Outreach Team contacted local non-profits to see how we can support access to sanitation and offer dignity to others. The AFC Shower Trailer, rotates among participating faith community shelter sites, and they are in need of towels and wash clothes. You can help! Bring a new towel and/or wash clothe to Peace UCC between now and Christmas Eve and leave it in the church office. Thank you all for generously support of Peace's efforts to raise awareness and funds though National Toilet Day. We raised $175.00 to date for Latrine Projects with Santa Cruz al Salvador.
