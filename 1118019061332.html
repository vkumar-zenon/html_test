Armando "Chick" Galella - Okinawa
Remembering Pearl Harbor
On December 7, 2019 we remember the 78th anniversary of the Japanese attack on Peal Harbor. In 2018 we interviewed Armando "Chick" Galella. Sgt. Major Galella was stationed at Hickam Air Base, adjacent to Pearl Harbor, when the second wave of the Japanese planes attacked. Nearly half of the airplanes at Hickam Field were destroyed or severely damaged. Hickam's casualties included 121 men killed, 274 wounded and 37 missing. Sgt. Major Galella served 5 years in the Pacific during WWII and contributed to the Battle of Okinawa.
If you have already watched Chick's story, please view it again as we recently found new footage of Okinawa and added it to the story
