My Pinnacle Network Snow Policy
Now that winter is almost upon us, people have asked about My Pinnacle Network's snow policy. Our guideline has always been if there is school in the town of the meeting, consider the meeting a go. If you are concerned or have questions about making a meeting when there's snow or bad weather in the forecast, please contact your group leader.

In the event of a cancellation, we will do our best to reschedule the meeting.

This winter, please take road conditions into consideration when planning your trip to My Pinnacle Network meetings. It's another reason why planning to get to the meeting 15 minutes before the start is a good idea.
