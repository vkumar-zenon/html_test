Project LifeSaver of SLO County
Needs South County Volunteers!

 
Project LifeSaver is a partnership of the SLO Lions Clubs, SLO County Sheriff's Office and Senior Volunteer Services.
 
PLS locates adults/children at risk of wandering. A transmitter wristband is provided with a battery-operated radio that emits an automatic tracking signal every second, 24 hours a day. The signal can be tracked on the ground or in the air via satellite over several miles.
 
Volunteers are critical as they play a major role in placing the device with PLS clients and provide needed equipment maintenance. 
 
To volunteer, please contact Lisa Marie Donnahoo at 805-781-5484 or by e-mail: Ldonnahoo@co.slo.ca.us

United Cerebral Palso-SLO 
CIP Tours 2020
 
United Cerebral Palsy-SLO has monthly CIP (Community Interaction Programs) has chartered vacations to interesting places such as Disneyland, Monterey, sporting events and many more location just for UCP members!
 
We rely on volunteers to help make these tours functional and meaningful. As a volunteer you will bond with our members, travel to fun places and enjoy a unique experience. Each tour includes transportation, lodging, meals and activities for volunteers. Personal spending money is not included.
