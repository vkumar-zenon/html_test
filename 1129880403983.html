October 29 Bus Tour

Twenty-four WGC members and guests were treated to a bus tour of York City's newest projects, led by YCEA President Kevin Schreiber and YCEA Vice President Silas Chamberlin.

Additional speakers included Danielle Stehman, Architect with Buchart Horn; Terri Altland, Director of Development at the York County History Center; Dr. Pamela Gunter-Smith, President of York College of PA; Jeff Vermeulen, Executive Director of York College's J.D. Brown Center for Entrepreneurship; Elaine Bonneau, Director of Downtown Inc.

As you can see, the group had a wonderful time!
Prospective and New Member Orientation

Sixteen prospective members joined current members on November 19 to learn about the Women's Giving Circle's history, our approach to grant making, and our commitment to educating our members to be effective strategic philanthropists. Nancy Ahalt, Victoria Connor, and Sue Sedam provided information and fielded questions.

Four attendees have already joined - we welcome Nina Birenberg, Dr. Pamela Gunter-Smith, Megan Lecas, and Maria Royce!
