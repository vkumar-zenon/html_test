RABUN COUNTY LAKE SALES REPORT

November 2019
The Sun Preparing to Set on 2019  
   


2019 is coming to a close, and at a glance, you might think that lake sales have stalled. Indeed, the number of transactions on all lakes was down in 2019 compared to 2018. However, there are a few points to note:

  • 2018 was an EXTRAORDINARY year in terms of the number of sales
  • The inventory for 2019 was a low as we have seen it in many years
  • The 2019 average sales price on all lakes was higher than in previous years
  • The days on the market in 2019 were also lower than in past years
The lake market continues to be very well-paced, with newly listed properties going under contract and several new properties hitting the market. Inventory is still somewhat limited, and we continue to see multiple offers on well-priced properties and a shortening of days on the market. The holiday months are usually slower as buyers turn their attention to retail shopping. Depending on the severity of the winter, most buyers will re-emerge in mid to late January, looking for properties to settle on before the summer season.

If you are considering selling your home, now is a great time to ask for a market analysis of your property, so please give us a call.

LAKE BURTON

There have been 44 sales on Lake Burton so far in 2019, with five more properties pending purchase. At this time in 2018, there had been 53 sales and five pending sales, so we area bit behind 2018. However, the average days on the market for 2019 is 218 compared to 912 at this time last year, and the average sales price is almost 5% greater than in 2018.  We feel the 2019 sales are only lower than in 2018 because the current inventory level is continuing to shrink, and the choice of desirable properties is lessening. We have several active buyers just waiting for the right fit.

LAKE RABUN

So far, in 2019, fourteen properties have sold. At this time in 2018, 24 properties had sold, and 4 more were under contract. At the present, of the 21 remaining properties, 15 of them are either tear downs or vacant lots, leaving only six move-in-ready homes for sale. Similar to Lake Burton, the average days on the market is shrinking -- for 2019 it is 288 compared to 601 at this time in 2018. Also, the average sales price is up significantly from 2018. The demand continues to be VERY strong, and prices are rising slowly due to the shrinking inventory.
