Happy Holidays! I hope you are enjoying the season and that you are healthy, happy and living with joyful intention. You will hear many say, "it's a time of letting go", and/or, "it's a time of cleansing, a time to release, to renew, to restart, etc.". All of these ideas are connected to the newness of the year that is coming and the ending of the year that is passing. We are drawn to these ideas because they "trigger" us to pay attention to the parts of our lives that are important while reminding us to stop giving so much attention to those things that are not part of our growth, our joy, and our positive intentional lifestyles.

So, I ask you:
Do you want to be happy?
Do you want to stop doing the things that do not make you feel good?
Do you really want to heal, grow, and learn?
... Well, Do You?

You may have answered yes to those questions ; you may have felt angry, defensive, or confused by those questions. Whatever your responses, pay attention to them!
And, know this:
  • YOU are the only one who can make changes in your life.
  • YOU have to do things differently if you want to make those changes.
Creating change is challenging, and scary , and may seem impossible. But, I know that once you get started, you can do it.

How to get started?
  • Make of list of changes you want to make.
  • Get started on the first thing on the list.
  • Do it right now. Once you take even the smallest action, you will begin to feel, see, and experience change.

So, as mentioned above: This IS the perfect time of year to start focusing on letting go of whatever it is you want to let go of; and, it is the perfect time of year to start working toward that which will make you happy.
Enjoy the rest of your holiday season and have a glorious New Year!
