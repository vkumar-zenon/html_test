Post Induction Therapy Psycho-Educational Series
What I Didn't Know Then ... It's Not Too Late
Facilitated by Catherine Mollner, MA, LP, SEP

We do not always learn everything we need to know about navigating life's challenges and growing from struggles during our childhood. Valuing and creating a healthy relationship with self is important so we can operate from a position of authenticity and empowerment 

This 4-session series will help you understand how earlier life experience affects your current life and develop strategies for being your best self. 
 
  • Learn how to connect to the truth of your inherent value and distinguish it from the tyranny of hustling to gather worth externally.
 
  • Learn a system of boundaries that will help you protect yourself while also learning how to not be offensive to others.

 
  • Practice scanning and asking "what is going on in my body (senses), emotions (feelings), thoughts and behavior? This strategy is about self-awareness.
 
  • We will discuss how to tend to our needs and ask for help when we need it. We will also talk about the importance of cultivating joy through meeting some of our wants.

 
  • We will explore balance and moderation.

  • Your History is Showing - Why we can know better but struggle to do better. We will discuss strategies for Surfing the Wave of Activation.

Whats new with Procentive?
Supported Browser Changes for Procentive 

In January, 2020, Procentive will expand our support of internet browsers to four instead of the current three. 
To provide a positive experience for our customers as we make improvements to Procentive using current web technologies, we'll be supporting the following browsers as of January 1, 2020:

* Chrome
*Safari
*Firefox
*Edge Chromium*-
Support will begin its launch date, anticipated on January 15th, 2020.


Note: Although Internet Explorer 11 will likely work with much of Procentive in the near future, we will not be supporting its use. Unfortunately, Internet Explorer has not kept up with a number of advancements in web technologies, including changes in styling (how a site looks the way it does) and scripting (how a site calculates what should be displayed). 

If you are a current user of Internet Explorer, please try one of the other browsers prior to January 1, 2020, so you are ready to make the switch. You may be pleasantly surprised by the experience! 

Helpful Hints



Please remember to refresh your browser every once in a while through the day! Without doing so Procentive will not always be on real time and you may not notice your client has checked in even though they actually have!
