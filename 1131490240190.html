Oversight, Transparency, & Public Management Subcommittee:

At the beginning of the Interim Committee Weeks, I was placed on the Oversight, Transparency & Public Management Subcommittee. During our October meeting we were able to hear four Proposed Committee Bills dealing with the Open Government Sunset Review Act and HB 195 regarding Public Records. The Proposed Committee Bills were brought forth to ensure certain public record exemptions remain in place, as the exemptions have to be reviewed every fifth year. The Bills would ensure that certain personal information obtained by departments (such as email addresses/financial information/information in an investigation) would remain confidential. House Bill 195 prohibits an agency that receives public record request from filing civil action against the entity making the request. As we meet in the coming weeks, I look forward to hearing other pieces of legislation that promote transparency in our great State!
