SquadLocker - Updated
With New Swag Options and Open for Ordering
 
 
Hey Ice Cats SquadLocker is now open and has been updated with lots of new swag options!
 
New branding options include UA, Adidas and The NorthFace and most gear may be personalized to include a player name and number.
 
Log in via the link below or navigate to Walter Johnson Ice Hockey under Find Your Store
 
https://teamlocker.squadlocker.com/#/lockers/walter-johnson-ice-hockey

SquadLocker often runs discount and shipping sales, check the site frequently as the sale details will be noted at the top of the page.
 
What a great way to support the Club, not only sporting cool swag, but the Club earns fundraising money for all merchandise sold.
