More about PWHub 

PWHub is the only county wide cross sector network in Oxfordshire dedicated to support the career success, fulfillment and personal growth of senior employed women. PWHub began in March 2016 and we have quarterly events 6-8pm in March, June, September and December. We vary the venues across Oxfordshire to be inclusive of women in the county.
