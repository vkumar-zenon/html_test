USPTO Closed On December 24 and 25, 2019
   
 
Dear Clients:

The U.S. Government today has declared December 24, 2019, to be a Federal Holiday.  Thus, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) and our office will be closed on Tuesday, December 24, 2019.  

As a result, any due dates for filing papers in the USPTO that fall on December 24, 2019, will be due on December 26, 2019 (December 25, 2019, is a National Holiday and the USPTO is closed as well).  We recommend that documents with a deadline falling on December 24, 2019, be forwarded to us as soon as possible, so that we may file the same by Monday, December 23, 2019.  Otherwise, documents that are due on December 24, 2019 will be filed on December 26, 2019.
