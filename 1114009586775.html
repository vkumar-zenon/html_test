5 Year Anniversary
This month we have the privilege of congratulating three Securicon'ers for 5 years of service!
  • Alex Waitkus, in the Consulting Services Division-Southeast Region,
  • Brian Credeur, also in the Consulting Services Division-Southeast Region, and
  • Funsho Aiyedogbon, in the Risk Management Services Division, shared by both branches.
I know I say this nearly every time, but I truly can't believe five years has passed for either of them.  When we began acknowledging five years of service, there were only a couple of people even within thinking of that range. Now we have a single month with three people to acknowledge; each of whom have hung with us, supported their customer(s) and been loyal team members for half a decade.

I've said it before, that one of my favorite things about a small company is that everyone has the ability to make a difference; change the course of the company. Over the last five years, each of you have made a difference; been a shining star for our customers and created praise from your customers, not just for yourselves, but with yourselves as an example for Securicon. I'm very happy and proud to tell you that to acknowledge your tenure, loyalty and hard work, you are each being awarded with 40 hours of Bonus PTO. I hope you are able to use it to enjoy family and well-deserved relaxation over the coming year.
 
Thank you Alex, Brian and Funsho!
Paul
