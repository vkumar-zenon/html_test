A note from Adrianne


Dear Friends, 

As we draw yet closer to Christmas and the snow comes down, it is a delight to be warm inside. For those who ski, it is a delight to be out on the slopes and then warm inside. This
season is a delightful blend of snowy cold and homely warmth.

I hope you join us on this coming Sunday for our Family Christmas Sunday Pageant. This is a joyful opportunity for adults and children to absorb the Christmas spirit. As usual, the Service will be at 10:30am.

We will have a traditional service of Lessons and Carols on Christmas Eve at 7 pm. This service gives us an opportunity to join in singing beloved carols, listen to special music and hear once again the biblical account of a babe in a manger. We'll hear of shepherds, of choirs of angels and of mysterious wise men from the East who followed a star to Bethlehem.  The Service will conclude with the traditional lighting of candles as the lights dim. When all candles are lit, we will sing Silent Night and go forth in peace.

May you have a blessed and joyful Christmas.

Peace,
Adrianne Carr

Our annual Retired Clergy Offering this year will benefit the United Methodist Church's Preachers Aid Society and will take place on Sunday Dec. 22nd.

Consecration Sunday Update
We celebrated Consecration Sunday Sunday, November 17. We considered our theme this year of Welcoming, Accepting, Growing Together in God's Love by hearing from 1 Corinthians 13 about the challenge to grow in God's love. Our financial stewardship provides a community in which to exercise that growth in love through welcoming and accepting others. Praise God for whom many blessings flow! We have collected 29 pledge cards: including two new giving units! Thank you to everyone who has pledged to the continual support of UCU. May you commitment to our church community grow your faith in the coming year as we bring a light of welcome and acceptance our world!
If you haven't had a chance to turn in your pledge card, please mail it to Attn: Financial Secretary, United Church of Underhill, PO Box 265, Underhill, VT 05489. Our pledges help our Trustees as they build our 2020 budget where our pledges make up the majority of committed resources (63% in 2019). Thank you for your commitment to UCU.
