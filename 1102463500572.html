Misplaced Debit Card

If you have misplaced your debit card you can log in to the QOCU Mobile App and turn your debit card OFF. Then, if you find your debit card, you can log back in to the QOCU Mobile App and turn the card back ON.

This "ON / OFF" feature allows you to avoid calling the 800 number and permanently closing your account while you look for the missing card.

Follow these 3 easy steps:
  1. ï»¿Log in to QOCU Mobile Banking App
  2. Click on the "CARDS" Icon
  3. Switch the card "on" or "off"
