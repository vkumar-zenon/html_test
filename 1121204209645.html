Asheville homeowners: we're accepting applications for YouthBuild Home Repair & Weatherization Services


GO's YouthBuild program is offering FREE weatherization and basic home repair services this year. Are you a homeowner in Asheville's Southside, Shiloh, East End or Burton Street neighborhoods? If you would like to increase the energy efficiency of your house or need some basic repairs completed, please contact us to discuss your project!

GO services will include:
- Basic home maintenance & repairs
- Basic airflow & heat loss assessments
- Window & door energy efficiency assessments & corrections
- Caulking where necessary 
- Weather stripping & insulation where necessary
- Lightbulb Assessment
- Replacement of incandescent bulbs with CFLs or LEDs
- Inspection of insulation in attic & crawl space/basement (if possible)
- Replacement of insulation as needed up to allotted material costs

Contact Eric Howell today to see if you qualify for this free service. Call (828) 398-4158 x118 or email Eric@greenopportunities.org. You'll need to complete a short application online or by phone to determine your eligibility.
