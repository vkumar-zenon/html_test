Trash and Recycle Service

Due to the New Year holiday falling on Wednesday RAD will be moving your trash and recycle day to Thursday next week.

Please note that your bulk recycle day has not changed. It is on the the second Wednesday of the month, January 8. Bulk pick-up is a good opportunity to dispose of your Christmas trees and other bulk items, such as large appliances (excluding refrigerators). Please contact RAD ( 480-983-9100) to schedule your bulk pick-up. Bulk trash pick-up must be scheduled before 12 pm on the day prior to your area pick-up date.

Celebrate Good Times
'Tis the season to celebrate and for many of you that means with fireworks. The Town of Queen Creek allows the Use of Permissible Fireworks between December 24 - January 3. According to the Town's website, "It is important to know that consumer fireworks are allowed only on private property and a person(s) who use, discharge or ignite permissible consumer fireworks are responsible and liable for expenses as a result of any emergency response that is required by such use, discharge or ignition per the Town Code."
For more information, please reference  Arizona State Law related to consumer fireworks .
