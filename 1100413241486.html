Healthy RC Honors Community Members' Service
From top left to bottom left: Manuela Amaya and Natalie Masachi, and top right to bottom right: Dr. Emily Shum and Nanette Hart.
Since 2008, Healthy RC has celebrated many successes in large part due to the dedication and commitment of the community members who give of their valuable time, effort, and expertise. Members of the Healthy RC Steering Committee have dedicated thousands of hours to improving the health of our Rancho Cucamonga residents. These community partners have been instrumental in the establishment of policies and implementation of various programs.
 
Healthy RC honors four community members with Outstanding Leadership Awards and a Compassionate Community Award. These individuals were selected for their work in the 2019 calendar year focusing on the goals and strategies of Healthy RC that benefit all Rancho Cucamonga residents. During the Healthy RC End-of-Year Celebration on December 9, 2019 the following community members were recognized:

  • Manuela Amaya, Campeones Para La Comunidad
  • Dr. Emily Shum, Healthy RC Partner
  • Natalie Masachi, Healthy RC Youth Leaders
  • Nanette Hart, Compassionate Community Award
 
Manuela Amaya has been a part of the Campeones Para La Comunidad (Community Champions) since 2016. She has done an incredible job carrying out the Healthy RC Quality of Life Surveys to identify needs of our community. Her passion for community outreach is unmatched because of her outgoing personality and love for serving her community. 
 
Since 2018, Dr. Emily Shum has consistently contributed her expertise as a mental health professional to support Healthy RCâs effort in destigmatizing mental health. This year, she received the Outstanding Leadership Award as a Healthy RC Partner because of her instrumental collaboration with Healthy RC's Mental Health Symposiums, Annual Teen Summit, and the Steering Committee.

Natalie Masachi is a 10 th grade student at Rancho Cucamonga High School and has been in the Healthy RC Youth Leaders since 2018. She immediately displayed outstanding leadership skills volunteering for various projects, speaking at meetings and events, as well as contributing to a mental health awareness video that won 1 st place in the state last year.
 
Finally, Healthy RC honored Nanette Hart for the Compassionate Community Award for truly embodying the essence of kindness and compassion. Her service at Los Amigos Elementary School and the Compassionate Community initiative has certainly helped create a more inclusive culture in the City of Rancho Cucamonga.

Evolution Fresh Joins Green Business Recognition Program

Evolution Fresh has joined the City of Rancho Cucamongaâs Green Business Recognition Program which recognizes businesses who have demonstrated a commitment towards environmental sustainability. Evolution Fresh, located on Jersey Boulevard, is a food manufacturing plant for cold pressed juices. They have focused their sustainable efforts specifically in landfill waste reduction, recycling, water conservation, energy efficiency, and partner engagement. Their waste reduction efforts in 2019 included diverting approximately 21,000,000 lbs. of organic waste (fruit and vegetables) to a hog farm. For water conservation, Evolution Fresh is on track to save 600,000 gallons of water by adjusting their food manufacturing line this calendar year. To maximize energy efficiency, a significant investment to retrofit the entire site with LED lights was completed over a multi-year period. 
 
For partner engagement, Evolution Fresh is currently instituting the Green Apron certification program, an electronic training platform, that educates employees about various sustainability and eco-conscious programs. They also work collaboratively with upstream and downstream partners to ensure environmental sustainability practices are in place. The City is delighted to have Evolution Fresh participate in the Green Business Recognition Program.  Their efforts to reduce organic waste and conserve water in their manufacturing process have been truly impressive. For more information about joining the Green Business Recognition Program please contact Deborah Allen, Management Aide at (909) 774-2047 or Deborah.Allen@CityofRC.us .
Be a Sustainability Hero!
Support renewable energy efforts in our community by considering going Solar, at NO COST to you!

Claudia Paz and her husband recently applied online with the non-profit organization, Grid Alternatives, which offers a no-cost solar program for income qualified families. They had heard about the City partnership with Grid Alternatives from one of their neighbors and was very interested in the program. At the start of the new funding cycle in October 2019, the Paz Family was the first family in Rancho Cucamonga to receive the complimentary solar-powered system!

Mr. and Mrs. Paz are both retired and are longtime homeowners in southwest Cucamonga. The solar panels were installed at the Paz residence in November 2019. The solar-powered system installed on their roof came at no cost and there were also no loans or liens associated with the improvement. The solar-powered system also comes with a ten-year warranty.

To find out if you meet the eligibility requirements for the Grid Alternatives program, please call (951) 465-2864 or visit www.gridsolar.org . You may also contact Deborah Allen, Management Aide at the City of Rancho Cucamonga at (909) 774-2047 or Deborah.Allen@CityofRC.us .

Caring is Always in Season
Healthy RC and the City of Rancho Cucamonga would like to recognize a community partner that has gone above and beyond in serving the community. Three years ago, City Council proclaimed the City as a Compassionate Community to inspire a culture of kindness and inclusivity and we wish to celebrate the efforts of community members who are impacting the lives of those who live, work, and play in this City. During the November 20, 2019 City Council Meeting, Judy Alvarenga , a local store manager of Starbucks was recognized because of her outstanding leadership in creating an inclusive and supportive community through the Affirmation Mailbox at Starbucks. Her commitment to serving the community and passion for uplifting others is a true hallmark of a Compassionate Community member!
