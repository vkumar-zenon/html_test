Meet our superstar pup of the month, Sophia!


Introducing the 2019 
Pet Prescription Dog of the Year!

Sophia is one incredible pup! After training at It's A Dog's World, she went on to another facility to receive her therapy dog certification. At the certification facility, she was attacked in the parking lot. While some thought that this meant the last of her therapy dog days, she proved she had the heart of a fighter and persevered. Once she healed nearly a year later, she received her official certification and is working with her owner to this day. Her story inspired the folks at Pet Prescription where she was named Dog of the Year. We are so proud of you, Sophia! It was a joy training you and we're thrilled you went on to earn your therapy dog certification.

Tis the season for holiday cheer and spreading joy to all - including your pet! As the year comes to an end and you spend time with friends, family, and fur babies, take a moment to ensure your surroundings are safe for your dog. Whether traveling to places with colder weather or enjoying time in your backyard, make sure there is nothing dangerous hiding in the snow or grass. Broken holiday lights or hazardous food can sometimes fall to the wayside and be in direct sight of your pet. Don't forget to do a quick sweep of your surroundings before relaxing for the day.
