Year End Thanks in Pictures 
It seems right to end the year with thanks. Thank you for praying and supporting us through another year of fruitful ministry.  As we reflect on 2019 we are thankful... 
 
For the Berlin Prayer Journey  
This year's Prayer Journey was a great success.  Here are just some snippets from the feedback we got: "The Lord spoke life into some waste places of my heart that have been plaguing me for years." "I felt God retaught me the power and importance of prayer." "I felt God confirmed a call for me and my wife for long- term missions."
For Baptisms  
We are thankful for the baptism of one of the refugees to whom we've been ministering (below) and three of the Master's Commission students (above).  It was moving to hear the story of this refugee again, his suffering, his sacrifice to follow the Lord, and his sense of the overwhelming majesty of our King. 


For Eowyn's Studies
We are thankful that Eowyn has now completed the requirements to enter the Doctor of Ministry program at Covenant Seminary in January. She hopes to be sharpened for further ministry to the German church. Here she is being interviewed in front of a youth group.
For Humble and Emerging Leaders
We are thankful for the leaders with whom David has served as International Director.  What a godly, humble and wise group of men and women.  We are also thankful for the new leaders who God is raising up for the years to come.  This picture shows leaders who are retiring praying over the newly appointed leaders to Europe.
For Our Partnering Church in Berlin 
We are thankful for the Luke Church - for our partnership with them; for the opportunies they give David to preach, to teach theology for the Master's Commission and Eowyn to serve refugees.  Here is a link to David's last sermon for those who know a bit of German!
