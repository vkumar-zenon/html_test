Crystal didn't grow up in a safe or supportive environment, and after only four years of schooling, she had to teach herself how to read and write.
 
"Nobody ever believed in me except for my brother," Crystal told us. "He knew I was smart. He made me promise to sit down and teach myself how to do it. He gave me a dictionary and said, 'Here you go. Get to writing.'"
 
Crystal kept writing, and wrote a lullaby, "Close Your Eyes Little Baby."
 
You turned a lullaby into a legacy.
 
"I used to be an emergency foster parent for babies that were left at the fire station." Crystal explained. "I would clean them up, put them in some warm clothes, and sing them my lullaby. I've watched hundreds of babies over the years, and my lullaby has never failed."
 
Thanks to you, Crystal achieved her final Dream of bringing her lullaby to life. Singer Essence Goldman and her production teammate Daniel Berkman took Crystal's lyrics and sheet music and produced a recording, and you also helped us provide CDs of her song complete with cover art and handcrafted cards.
 
Friends and family planned a party for Crystal to hear her lullaby for the first time. She was overjoyed. "Thank you so very, very much to you and everyone who helped. It was made from the heart."
