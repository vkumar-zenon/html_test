""" HART Bus and Streetcar New Year's Holiday Service Schedule

HART and the TECO Line Streetcar New Year's Holiday Service Schedule
Streetcar service extended until 2 AM; bus service free after 7 PM on NYE
TAMPA, Fla., - The Hillsborough Area Regional Transit Authority (HART) will operate all fixed-route bus service on a regular weekday service schedule on Tuesday, December 31, 2019. As customers celebrate the start of a new year, HART will offer free rides beginning at 7:00 PM on all services, including paratransit.  The hours on bus and van services will not be extended; please check HART bus schedules for the last trip on each route.

To help support a safe alternative to driving on New Year's Eve, the TECO Line Streetcar System will operate extended service until 2 AM.  


Please keep in mind, HART's safe, reliable, and frequent transit options.

 

HART will operate on a Sunday/Holiday schedule on Wednesday, January 1, 2019, in observance of New Year's Day. Regular weekday HART service will resume on Thursday, January 2, 2019. 

 

The TECO Line Streetcar System will operate on a normal Wednesday schedule on January 1, 2019. 


Customers should be aware of the following hours and schedules for HART Retail Sales Centers:    

  • On New Year's Eve, the customer service centers at  Marion Transit Center (MTC) will be open from 6 a.m. to 6 p.m., and at the University Area Transit Center (UATC)  from 6 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • HART Retail Sales Centers at UATC, MTC, and all other Authority offices will close on Wednesday, January 1, 2020, in observance of the holiday.
  • HART offices will return to regular business hours on Thursday, January 2, 2020.

HART Customer Service (813-254-4278) will be open on Tuesday, December 31, 2019, from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m., and from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Tuesday, January 1, 2020.


HARTPlus subscription customers who wish to use HARTPlus services on holidays must make a reservation through Customer Service at 813-254-4278 or TDD (813) 626-9158.
