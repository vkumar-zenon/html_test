ALL in the Community

Arlington Little League salutes members of and partners in our sports community who are earning well-deserved accolades for their work on behalf of Arlington youth.
  • Arlington Soccer Association Coach and Sports Commission Member John Mingus has been named SoccerAmerica's 2019 Volunteer of the Year.
  • Longtime 50/70 Athletics Coach Mike Goldsmith is the new Head Varsity Coach at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science & Technology.
  • Youth Umpire Sean Bender-Prouty was honored by Arlington's Human Rights Commission with the James B. Hunter Human Rights Outstanding Educational Individual Award. Giving back to your community isn't necessarily extraordinary, but sometimes it takes extraordinary strength and courage to do what is right and good. Make no mistake about it, Sean is an extraordinary person. You can watch this presentation online.
Congratulations, ALL! We're proud to call you family!

Primetime Baseball (5380 Eisenhower Ave, Alexandria) -- A local sponsor, instrumental to our offseason and preseason indoor baseball efforts, Primetime is hosting a College Recruitment Seminar tonight! Pete Wilk, Head Baseball Coach at Georgetown University, will be speaking about the recruiting process, steps needed to be seen by college coaches, and what it takes to play at the collegiate level. He will answer any questions about the process and what you as a parent/player need to do in each year of your high school career. The event is $10 per person 15-years of age and older. 14 and under are free. If you have any players wanting to play at the next level, this is a can't miss event. Email for info.
