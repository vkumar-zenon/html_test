Alignment Rockford Celebrates 10 Years!
In November, we had the pleasure of celebrating 10 years of making a positive impact in our schools and our community. Our current and past Executive Directors were on hand to blow out the birthday candles and surrounded by local and regional business leaders.

Rockford Mayor Tom McNamara also honored individuals with keys to the City for their passion and impact for and with Alignment Rockford.

HOST A JOB SHADOW

Job Shadowing links schools and businesses by providing students with hands-on, real-world experiences in their desired career field.

Recently, two auburn students shadowed Rockford Police Department District 2 for a ride-along to complete their 4-hour job shadow.

Additionally 36 students completed their hands-on job shadow at Woodward (shown below), Collins Aerospace, Hulsebus Chiropractic, and Header Die and Tool.

IncubatorEDU: Calling all Coaches, Mentors and Advisors!

Earlier this fall, Alignment Rockford launched the IncubatorEDU program which is a class that provides students with the opportunity to launch real businesses.

While students create and fully develop their own product or service, local entrepreneurs and business experts help guide them through the process and assist them with foundational business topics.

The students' interest in this has been great and we've already gained several awesome leaders to participate. We're seeking additional subject matters to be coaches for our students as well as mentors to provide strategic guidance.

Earlier this year, Alignment Rockford launched the Pathways Sponsorship Program which allows businesses to interact with RPS 205 students face-to-face, providing valuable opportunities to learn directly from experts in their field of interest and build the foundation for a successful career.
