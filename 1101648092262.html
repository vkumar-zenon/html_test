Meet Kizzy
Kizzy is a gorgeous 5-year-old Mastiff/Rottweiler mix looking for her forever home. This sweetheart was brought to us by our cruelty investigation team. As far as we know, Kizzy has lived outdoors for the majority of her life. She may need a little extra time and patience from her adopter as she settles in to her new home. Kizzy adores attention and seems to think she is a lap dog, as she will gladly curl up on your lap and gently nudge you for some attention. She can be somewhat shy at first and appreciates when new people go slow with her and allow her to come to them on her terms. Kizzy will need help in her new home with basic obedience and manners, and will also benefit from positive reinforcement training. Kizzy will do best in a home without any children, and would like to meet any of your currents dogs here at the shelter before adoption.

Meet Olive
Olive is a beautiful dilute tortoiseshell who was brought to our shelter by a caring person who found her injured in their yard. Olive has recovered well and is now looking for a home to call her own. We don't know much about Olive's past, but she has proven to be a super sweet girl both in the shelter and in her foster home. She will greet you with a loud "meow" as soon as she sees you and will wait (mostly!) patiently to be petted or brushed. Despite being a senior girl of 12, she does like to play! Her favorite toy is a stuffed mouse. We don't know about her previous interactions with other animals, so we recommend slow introductions to any resident pets. 

Ask about Olive at our Quakertown shelter or call us at 267-347-4674.
