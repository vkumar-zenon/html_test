World Handicap System
The Iowa Golf Association (IGA) has announced use of the new World Handicap System in conjunction with the USGA and GHIN Products platform. This major revision to the handicapping system is scheduled to launch January 6, 2020.
  • Six (6) major changes are incorporated with the World Handicap System. I encourage members to view highlights on the IGA website iowagolf.org/whs/. I will be attending tutorial handicap seminars and workshops to accommodate the membership for 2020.
  • The IGA has not increased the handicap fee for 2020. It remains $25 and is optional however, if you plan on playing FDCC events, other outside events or IGA sanctioned tournaments a valid and current IGA handicap is required.
  • FDCC Members with 2019 handicaps - Handicap posting will remain active through mid-March at which time you will become "Inactive" until; (1) You have paid for your 2020 handicap, then you will remain active for 2020 otherwise (2) You will remain "Inactive" until the 2020 Handicap fee is paid in full.
FYI - In 2019, FDCC had 90 individuals with IGA handicaps
