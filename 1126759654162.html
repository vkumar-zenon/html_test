How to fight those "Holiday Blues"

As the daylight hours shorten during the winter and the days are colder we can find our life rhythm to be a bit off. Which can even include our sleep patterns. This has a domino effect on the quality of the rest of our lives.

Some people struggle with anxiety, seasonal depression or compounding stress more in the winter than other times of year. Add in the joy and routine of family holidays and traditions - it can result in rushing through the season wishing for it to be over. And what a bummer that is!

We don't want to start the new year depleted, exhausted and even perhaps with a few battle wounds. So, let's get ahead of it - and maybe help a neighbor or friend along the way! So how do we go through this season equipped for healthy minds, bodies and happy hearts? What can you do TODAY and every day for the rest of 2019 to reverse that narrative and flip that script?

Some tips to help enjoy this season:

  • Eat your fruits & veggies! Drink your Shakeology! Add in Power Greens boost too for good measure! Your Shakeology will help with gut health which has direct links with melatonin production (sleep!) - serotonin / oxytocin (happy hormones) to prevent depression and anxiety. Plus those adaptogens in Shakeology will also help mitigate stress and lower cortisol levels!

  • Press play!! A new Harvard study found that as little as 150 minutes (that's only five 21 day fix workouts) of exercise a week can dramatically cut heart disease risk! It also is a great outlet for stress and will release endorphins that will elevate your MOOD and expand your bandwidth for mitigating stress.

  • Make plans with friends and family - and keep them! Plan / schedule connection and fun time, make it something YOU love to do! Try to put your phone away, be present and make memories!

  • Bundle up and get moving outside! Get outside in the fresh air and let your circadian rhythm sync. Breathing fresh air can stimulate your brain, oxygenate your blood and help your immune system to fight off disease more effectively. It also supplies your immune system with the oxygen it uses to kill and destroy bacteria, viruses and germs!

  • Sit by the window to boost your mood with natural light, park at the far end of the parking lot, take the stairs and look for other creative ways to keep your body moving. How simple is that?!

  • Make sure you're getting enough SLEEP! Sleeping less than 7 hours a night has a direct impact on your body's production of hunger / satiation hormones and our body's ability to process glucose! All of which can result in a hangrier, crankier and more sluggish YOU!

  • Make hot tea/cocoa, cozy up with a blanket, pet or loved one. As you know - I'll always advocate for snuggling up with a pup or kitty!! ;)

  • Lastly, adjust your expectations. If every holiday event seems to end up adding more stress then joy, then perhaps evaluating your expectations would be helpful. Do the cookies need to be perfectly iced, or is it more important to be creative together? Find the gratitude in the mayhem, be present and share your smile!

And remember - you are part of an army of light sharers and hope dealers (who also share the same struggles as you do) and are here to help every step of the way!

You are loved and never ever alone! Not when you're part of the Wild Reign Collective family, that is!

With hugs, Jamie Sheppard
