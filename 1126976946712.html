Wee Watch Illness Policy

Wee Watch Licensed Home Child Care requires parents and providers to follow these health regulations to help keep the incidences of colds, flu and contagious illness to a minimum. These policies and procedures are established and enforced with concern for the well being of all children in the day care home.
Providers must inform the agency if they themselves or a family member experience any of these symptoms so that back-up arrangements can be made, if necessary.

1.    If your child has the following symptoms, please keep them at home and advise your provider of their absence.                             
-an elevated temperature, flushing, pallor
-nasal discharge, acute cold, or severe coughing
-vomiting or diarrhea
-lice or scabies
-unusual behaviour- irritable, fussy for no reason, listless
-undiagnosed spots or rashes, severe itching
-headache, stiff neck
-difficult or rapid breathing, sore throat or trouble swallowing
-earache, discharge from ear

2.    If the child displays these symptoms while at the daycare home, please be aware that your provider will call to request that you pick up your child.

3.  Parents must immediately pick up their child once notified by the provider and bring the child to their doctor, if necessary.
