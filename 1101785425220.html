Box Tops For Education Update  
Dovey Alexander

Starting this year, the Box Tops program is changing. Now, instead of clipping, checking expiration dates, bundling and submitting, Box Tops will be earned through the new mobile app.The app allows you to scan your store receipt. It finds the participating products for you and instantly adds Box Tops to our school's earnings online! You must scan your receipt within 14 days of purchase and each Box Top will still be worth $.10.

Learn more and download the app today! Box Tops are an easy way for our PTA to raise money for the important programs we sponsor.  Please continue to send in paper box tops if you have them.
