at this time of year
Have you ever wondered why certain spices and aromas are popular around Christmas?

Obviously, the Christmas Tree makes aromas such as Douglas Fir, White for or Siberian Fir popular at this time of year.

But, why citruses and cinnamon?

Interestingly enough, these essential oils are terrific for
 1) Boosting the Immune System
 2) Uplifting the mood
 3) Decreasing Stress and "Winter Blues."
 4) Keeping the air clean

Fir oils are also helpful for promoting better respiratory health and decreasing congestion.

Here's another interesting note: people who have allergies to firs have reported that they don't necessarily have sensitivities to the oil.

The reason is a little simple: the part of the plant someone is allergic to isn't present in the oil, so someone can enjoy the scent of a fir tree by diffusing the fir oil, even if they're sensitive to the actual tree.

So, here are a few essential oils and essential oil blends that you can diffuse throughout your house, make it smell like Christmas, boost you and your family's immune system, uplift your mood, decrease stress and keep the air clean:
