Hamidou's Update

Hamidou is a Senior Public Relations major at SUNY Oswego

"This semester was definitely a test of my mental strength and growth as a college student. This past semester at Oswego, I took 5 upper division classes that are very important for my major in public relations, as well as an internship for the SUNY Oswego Instagram page. I had to post events and hidden gems at Oswego for prospective students that are interested in the institution. The internship technically served as a sixth class because I was granted three credits. Having an overload of six classes definitely served as a challenge for me because there were moments I struggled to complete all of my work and assignments. It was tough, but I learned from my mistakes and managed to finish my work and average a B for the semester. I give thanks to God and the support of my friends and family."

Cheick's Update:

Cheick is a 2nd year law student at the Benjamin Cardozo School of Law in NYC

"I have had a challenging and yet rewarding experience this past semester at Cardozo Law. I took classes in Copyright Law, Constitutional Law, and the Rules of Evidence. I found my class in Constitutional Law the most rewarding. Constitutional Law is a fascinating and important topic as we are given a backstage look at how and why our country looks the way it does today. Law School can be stressful, but my experience as a 2L has equipped me with the knowledge and tools to take care of my mental health. I can't say I'm having the time of my life at law school but I am excited for a career in the legal field."
Nakkia's Update:
