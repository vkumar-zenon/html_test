Inclement Weather Policy

If Anderson County Schools cancel school due to inclement weather, all county 4-H afterschool and evening events are automatically cancelled.  This does not apply to regional and state events.

Congress Essay Theme Announced
 
The 72nd TN 4-H Congress will be held on March 21-25, 2020.  This is one of our oldest and most prestigious events!  There is a LOT that happens during this statewide event such as:
 
  • Mock legislation in the Capitol and visits with elected officials
  • State public speaking finals
  • Citizenship and leadership project interviews (portfolio competition)
  • Officer elections
  • History bowl contest
  • River boat cruise on the General Jackson
  • Plus tons of banquets, award ceremonies, dances and fun!
Youth can only attend as a Tennessee 4-H Congress delegate once, in either their 9th or 10th grade year. However, they can "win" a trip to the event as a leadership or citizenship portfolio finalist or as a public speaking finalist--potentially attending all four years as a senior high 4-H member! All of the delegates travel on chartered bus to the event. The cost to attend is approximately $325-$350 (including bus transportation).
 
In Anderson County, 2 full scholarships and one partial scholarship are awarded to outstanding 9th & 10th graders based upon their essay and portfolio.  Scholarship winners are selected as follows:
  • Congress Essay (Due January 15) - A 500 word essay on the theme, "Tennessee 4-H: A Place to Call Home." 50%
  • 4-H Portfolio (Due January 27) - A summary of a member's experiences in their major project, 4-H leadership and service. 50%
Your 4-H staff would love to show you how to complete a portfolio. Questions? Email Donna Carter, 4-H Extension Agent.

Portfolio Help Sessions
 
A portfolio is a record of a member's project, leadership and service activities over several years.  Youth in 6th-12th grade are eligible to enter portfolio competition.  Winners in this competition earn scholarships, awards, trips and other prizes depending on their grade division.

Don't be frustrated and try to do the portfolio on your own.  We've added monthly 4-H Portfolio Help Sessions where youth and their parents can get hands-on help: 
  • Thursday, December 19 (1:00 - 7:00) *** Special Date/Time Added
  • Thursday, January 2 (4:00 - 7:30)
  • Thursday, February 6 (4:00 - 7:30)
  • Thursday, March 5 (4:00 - 7:30)
  • Thursday, April 2 (4:00 - 7:30)
Youth and their parents can attend for any part of these sessions.  A mobile computer lab will be set up so members can work on their portfolios; youth are also welcome to bring their own computer.  Please call or email at least a day in advance if you're coming, so we can have a computer set up for you.
