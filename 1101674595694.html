Asset of the month: Adult Role Models
Adult role models have a tremendous impact on young people. Teachers, coaches, relatives, community members, and other caring adult make a big impact.
What You Can Do
Smile and say hi to youth you encounter.
Model hard work, a positive attitude, and respect for others.
Dont be afraid to start a conversation with a young person in your neighborhood.
Demonstrate positive responses to difficult situations, such as offering a sincere apology when appropriate, or trying again when you fail to achieve a goal.
