Munroe Preserve Conservation Easement, Ossipee
The Dan Hole Pond Watershed Trust (DHPWT) recently conveyed a conservation easement to LRCT to ensure the protection of the Charles Norman Munroe Preserve forever.

LRCT will monitor the 177-acre Munroe Preserve regularly to ensure continuing protection of the conservation values, including its extensive wetlands and upland forests that are home to a wide variety of wildlife.

The two organizations look forward to their partnership in permanently preserving the Munroe Preserveâs spectacular habitat and making it available for public benefit and enjoyment.

Belknap Woods Expansion, Center Harbor
In October, Belknap Woods was substantially expanded to nearly 130 acres through an extraordinarily generous gift of an abutting parcel of land by the Mattson family of Center Harbor.

LRCT is proud to have partnered with the Squam Lakes Association (SLA), Squam Lakes Conservation Society (SLCS) and the Mattsons on this outstanding project.

SLA is now the owner of the former Mattson land, the SLCS conservation easement has been expanded to encompass this land, and LRCT holds a backup interest in the conservation easement on the entirety of Belknap Woods.
