A Gift-Giving Thought

PRESENTS or PRESENCE?

During the beautiful holiday season, many people sometimes feel anxious to find loved ones and dear friends the "perfect" gift because giving monetary gifts has long been considered a prime way to express love and caring.

 However, it is suggested that gestures do not need to have an expensive price tag to feel meaningful.

Small acts of kindness, not grand overtures, make people feel most loved, cherished, and supported.

While the idea of offering loved ones the gift of time and attention may pale in comparison to giving them a lavish present, empathy research shows that shared human experiences can tighten social bonds. 

And, that despite personality differences, most people agree about what makes us feel loved -- the presence of our loved ones and dear friends.



Merry Christmas!

Wishing you and yours all the joys, blessings and magical moments of this beautiful season.
