Cold Weather Reminder:
  With the cold weather here and the snow soon approaching, please make sure that your child is dressing appropriately for the cold weather.  Please label and send in heavy jackets, hats, gloves and scarves.  In accordance with DHS regulations, children go outdoors daily unless the wind chill is below 25 degrees.    
Also, please make sure that you check your child's extra clothes bin at the center to ensure that the extra set is weather appropriate.  

Snow Closing Procedures
  In regards to snow closures and delayed openings, MELC will contact you via One Call in which you will receive a text and email. 
 
Pajama Drive
Thank you to everyone that helped contribute to Room 11's Pajama Drive.  We surpassed our goal and collected 205 sets of pajamas for children and adults at the  Elwyn Darby PACT program in Darby!  Each person also received a book to go with the pajamas from Scholastic Books.
