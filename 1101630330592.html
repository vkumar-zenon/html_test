Looking for a Meaningful Way to Acknowledge your Child's Teacher at the Holidays?
Opt to give a Lower Merion Township Scholarship Fund Recognition Card in his/her name. By donating to the Scholarship Fund, you would be helping a student that might have been taught by your teacher or may go to help a classmate in the future!

LMTSF has been helping our high school graduates realize their college dreams for more than 50 years! Currently, 110 LMSD graduates are attending colleges with grants from the Scholarship Fund. These grants help defray the ever-increasing costs to attend college.

Recognition cards may be purchased through your child's school office or through the LMTSF website. Cards are $15 per card or $40 for 3 cards.
 
Consider supporting a LMSD graduate while honoring a special teacher, specialist, support staff or school administrator!

For additional information, please contact Mary Donoghue in the Scholarship Fund Office.
