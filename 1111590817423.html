NEW YEAR'S EVE - LAST BLAST
Old Orchard Beach has its own way of celebrating all year long and New Year's Eve will be no exception! The annual Last Blast New Year's Eve Beach Party will be the perfect way for you to honor and say farewell to 2019 before ringing in the new year. The festivities will begin at 3 PM in downtown OOB on December 31, 2019 and will continue until 8 PM.
Bring your appetite because there will be kettle corn, cotton candy, fire pits for toasting marshmallows, and the new Maine Kitchen Concessions which will have an array of tasty treats available! The bonfire will be lit at 4:30 PM and if you still have your Christmas Tree ... bring it down to feed the fire! The evening will conclude with a spectacular fireworks display at 7:30 PM. For more info, visit: OOB365
NEW YEARS DAY - LOBSTER DIP
If you will be staying with us at the Sea Cliff House during the New Year's Day holiday, start 2020 off right by doing something fun, exhilarating, fulfilling, and extremely beneficial for countless well-deserved individuals. The annual Lobster Dip raised over $100,000 in 2019 which provided funding for Special Olympics athletic training and support for thousands of Maine athletes with intellectual disabilities. The objective is to raise that and even more during the 2020 dip, which will be an all-day celebration from the dip itself to the post-dip party which will be held at The Brunswick with a free buffet, cash bar, and live music. The event will begin at 12 noon sharp in front of the The Brunswick (39 W. Grand Ave, OOB, ME) on January 1, 2020. Come one or come all as a participant or a spectator! For more info, visit:
