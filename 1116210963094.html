Youth Accomplishments
Currituck County 4-H Sharpshooters team members Kaitlyn Carey and Bode Fredrick have been selected to represent North Carolina as members of the 4 person, NC State Rifle Team.

Bode and Kaitlyn will compete with the team at the National 4-H Shooting Sports Tournament in Grand Island Nebraska on June 21-26, 2020.

Kaitlyn and Bode were alternates on the 2019 N.C. 4-H National Rifle team and traveled to Ellerbe, NC monthly in order to practice with the State Rifle team.

ROBO RAPTORS - LEGO EV3 & TECHNOLOGY CLUB

The Robo Raptors 4-H Club is a Robotics and Technology focused club. Meeting twice a month on the 2nd and 4th Fridays from 5:30 to 7:30. Youth work on various tasks including building and programming Lego EV3 Robots. We have recently begun using 3D printers. Youth are learning how to find designs, use specialized software and print creations. In this club we use teamwork for problem solving and to accomplish tasks set forth by club leaders. We are always looking for new members and new ideas.
