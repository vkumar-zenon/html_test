Social Media Post of the Week: "Rare Blue Lobster Sighting!"
When local purveyor Patriot Lobster shared an incredible "catch" on their Instagram account, we thought you all might love it as much as we did. And sure enough, you did! The one in a ten million (or something like that) blue lobster certainly caught your attention and we are happy to announce it as our Social Media Post of the Week.

The lobster reached more than 14,794 people and garnered 1,571 reactions! More than one thousand people liked it and 93 "loved" it. An incredible 109 followers shared the post with their friends, further spreading the word about this amazing sea creature.
