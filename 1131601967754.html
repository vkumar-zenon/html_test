Epiphany Donations
On Sunday January 5th we'll celebrate the feast of the Epiphany, which commemorates the visit of the wise men to the Baby Jesus with their gifts of Gold, Frankincense, and Myrrh. Tradition holds that others also came, bringing hot meals and diapers. In that spirit of gift giving we invite you to bring diapers and wipes for local babies in need. Bring them to church with you on Sunday January 5th and they will be donated to Foster Village to further our support of foster families. 
Deadline for 2019 Pledges and Contributions
The deadline for 2019 pledges and contributions is Sunday, Jan. 5th. Any pledges or contributions received after Sunday will be placed on the 2020 calendar year.
Daughters of the King
The Daughters of the King will meet at 1130am on the 4 th  Sunday of every month. We hope to gather at the back of the nave after fellowship until 12 noon. All are welcome. All women are invited to share, pray, and learn about DOK!!! 
Meditation Classes Resume January 8 th
During January, we will continue our focus on Personal Guardian Angels. We meet every Wednesday at 5pm in the Parish Hall. Meditation classes are lead by Mary Christopher who has more than 40 years of experience practicing and teaching meditation. If you have any questions, call Mary at 512-694-0643.
Find Your Flow Workshop - Jan. 11 th 10am-5pm, $45
This time of year we find ourselves making a list of resolutions for the New Year. Even if we don't write anything down, we think about what we would like to accomplish in the coming year. One of the drawbacks of our resolutions is what we leave out. For some reason, we omit the activities that bring us joy and fulfillment. Doing what brings us joy or at least enjoyment is one key to finding Flow. Being in our Flow is relaxing, often healing, and creative. Entire careers have been built by people finding their flow. Our Hearts can be restored to Joy. This is what we will do in our workshop. We will be Finding Our Flow for 2020. For questions and to sign up, call Mary at 512-694-0643
