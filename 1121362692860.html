You are INCREDIBLE!
It's hard to believe that just a few short weeks ago, we defied Hurricane Dorian and the vast majority of the 1313 cyclists who registered for our 12th Annual Ride actually showed up to ride! We braved the elements and celebrated our accomplishments as one big Ride Family. When the sun came out in the afternoon, the jubilation and sense of community was like none other.

Of course we could not have pulled it off without the 250+ volunteers who never gave a thought to cancelling. They showed up ready to serve and serve they did...from setting up the Ballpark to the Pre-Ride Dinner, Packet Pick-Up to Parking, Traffic, & SAG Vehicles, Rest Stops to the Post Ride BBQ, and everything in between! With a Ride staff of only one full-time and two part-time employees, the entire Ride Weekend is run under volunteer power! And what power that was!

As if all of those efforts weren't enough, every one of you, including nearly 100 Virtual Cyclists, put your hearts and souls into the real reason for the Ride, raising funds for research toward a cure. As of today, the 2019 New England Parkinson's Ride has raised an astounding $1,205,115!

With the help of our Sponsors, we are confident that we will reach our $1,250,000 goal!

Matching Donations For The
Entire Month of October
In yet another incredible act of generosity and commitment to finding a cure, our sponsors have agreed to match all donations, dollar for dollar, up to $50,000, from October 1 through October 31.

Now is the time to reach out to friends and family members who intended to donate but just never got around to it. Encourage them to give now to double the impact of their donations. $5 becomes $10 and $25 becomes $50. Imagine If each of us could find a way to raise just $35 more, we will earn the entire sponsor match, blowing the doors off of our $1.25 million goal! More importantly, every single dollar will fund the research that will improve the lives of those living with PD, and ultimately lead us to the cure!

*Match dollars will be allocated to the general ride fundraising page, as there is simply no way to match to individual fundraisers.
