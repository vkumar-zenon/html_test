Wreaths Across America

What a glorious, sun shine filled Saturday we had to lay the wreaths at St. Patrick's, Greenwood and Sierra Memorial Cemeteries. Yes, we met our goal and had wreaths left over to completely cover the Hooper Weaver site. We even had a dozen left so we took them to the Odd Fellows/Masonic cemetery off Kidder Street. Again, without your participation and help, this would not have been possible. I am deeply grateful to our Emmanuel congregation.

Wreaths Across America has announced that they will run a two for the price of one opportunity from now until January 15, 2020. If you have any spare change left over from Christmas, you might think about buying a couple of wreaths now for next year. I am sure we will be able to set a higher goal and you can help now. The online address is:

  https://www.wreathsacrossamerica.org/pages/20701
