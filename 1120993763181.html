Upcoming Webinar: New ISO 14644-3:2019 Cleanrooms and associated controlled environments Part 3 : Test Methods
 
I SO Technical Committee 209 (ISO TC209) published a new revision of ISO 14644-3 Cleanroom test methods. This second edition of ISO 14644-3 cancels and replaces the first edition (ISO 14644-3:2005), which has been technically revised.
The main changes compared to the previous edition are as follows:
- Installed filter system leakage test has been simplified and corrected to address concerns over its complexity and noted errors,
- guidance concerning classification of air cleanliness by airborne particle concentration has been moved to 14644-1:2015,
- the text of the whole document has been revised or clarified to aid in application.

This webinar will give you detailed information about revision and general implementation to exist cleanroom testing procedures.
