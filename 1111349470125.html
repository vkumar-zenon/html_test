RETURN ARTIST
Barbara Vanderbilt
November 5 - January 7
Artist and Tile Maker
 
This is a return exhibit for Vanderbilt who says of her work, "The beauty of the trees and rock formations on the coast of Maine is something that I can never get enough of. I love their shapes and textures and the stories they tell. I try to convey the interplay between strength and delicacy. The contrast between the peacefulness of the moment and the rough changes that nature brings in the way of seasonal variation, erosion and storm gives me endless material for painting and meditation."

CLYNK
Your empty 5 cent cans and bottles are worth cash for Gibbs Library.  It's an easy program.  Just pick up some green plastic CLYNK bags at the library, fill them with returnables and take them to any Hannaford store.  Read the directions on the bag carefully.  You'll need to place the sticker with the bar code onto the bag so the money can be credited to Gibbs.  Each bag is limited to 20 lbs.
