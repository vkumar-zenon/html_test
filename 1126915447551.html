""" KGC Staff Update on Sale to Purcell International Education
Purcell International Education to Proceed with Purchase

You are receiving this e-mail as you were an employee of the Kimberley Golf Club for the 2019 season. We would like to provide you with an update on the Sale of the golf course and what to expect for the 2020 golf season.

On Thursday morning Purcell International Education (PIE) formally notified the Kimberley Golf Club of their intent to proceed with the purchase the Kimberley Golf Club properties and assets.

The closing date of the sale will be January 13th 2020.

For the 2020 golf season this means that Purcell Golf Inc., a subsidiary of PIE, will take over all operational responsibility for the golf course, employees and facilities as of January 13th. The Kimberley Golf Club will continue to exist, however its function is limited to recruiting members and organizing events for the membership. Other than these changes, the golf club will operate much the same as in previous years, with a few improvements as described below.

Purcell Golf has extended offers of employment to each of the managers; Tim - Operations, Joanna - Food & Beverage, and Simon - Golf Shop, and we hope to see them continue on with Purcell Golf. Each manager will continue to hire their own staff as always.

What changes should you expect to see?

Purcell Golf has committed to investing in new equipment and course improvements. You should see some immediate replacement of equipment that urgently needs replacing. Once Purcell Golf has a change to evaluate the situation in more detail, there may be additional investment.

In the longer term, no more than 18 months from now, there will be a project initiated to build 4 new holes on the lower part of the property. On completion, these holes will replace holes 6, 7, 8, and 9, which PIE has identified as the location of the main campus for their school and related facilities.

Other activity will see the construction of a satellite campus located south of the maintenance shed and east of the first fairway. Building of this campus should be starting within the next year and will provide PIE the opportunity to open the first phase of their school in fall of 2021.

Look for more information to be released in January. We hope to see as many returning staff members as possible next summer.
