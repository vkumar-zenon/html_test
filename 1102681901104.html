NOVEMBER PLANETS
December 2: Jupiter enters Capricorn
As Jupiter joins major players Saturn and Pluto in this ambitious sign, focus on what you want to achieve. Pay attention to those areas of your life that you want to build. Hard work pays off and you may even come to someone's notice, a benefactor who will help you. Overall, although help arrives, this much focus on Capricorn can be problematic as the forces of those in charge consolidate their power. To succeed, your goals will need all of the concentration you can muster. Be practical, strategic and savvy. This is not time to wing it.

December 3: Venus sextiles Mars
The relationship planet, Venus, in commitment-focused Capricorn and the planet of action and desire, Mars, in determined Scorpio make a great team. This beneficial aspect helps you meet new people and strengthen existing relationships.

December 8: Sun squares Neptune
Neptune rules your dreams, fantasies, imagination, and spirituality making this a wonderful time to check out of your busy life, remove distractions, and relax. Answers are found not through logic but by daydreaming. You may not know where you are headed or what you want, but don't worry. All great minds need down time to replenish. Find a quiet place, relax, and breathe. Today, the world can wait.

December 9: Mercury enters Sagittarius
The communication planet in this optimistic sign gives you plenty of ideas and fun conversations that help you figure out how to reach your goals. If you keep your feet on the ground and mix practicality with your enthusiasm, you'll have the magical ingredients you need to succeed. Honesty is the top value of this sign, which can bring refreshing discussions. Just make sure you don't overshare since this planet-sign combo is known for statements followed by retractions. Be tactful and you won't have regrets.

December 11: Venus conjuncts Saturn
Saturn is interested in accountability. When these two join, your relationships are tested. The good news is that you get clarity about the people in your life and a firmer grasp on what you want and don't want.

December 12: Full Moon in Gemini
This information-loaded Full Moon brings news that makes you take a second look at your plans. Although you may feel overwhelmed, the rapid pace of this week can move you out of situations that are stale or outmoded. People are extra emotional now and much of what they say is contradictory. What should you do? Slow down and carefully review all of the scraps of data. Once you see the whole picture, you will know what you want to do.

December 13: Venus conjuncts Pluto
You have a lot of power and energy at your disposal. Let go of what's broken or not working unless it can be transformed into a new and better version. This includes, projects, goals and people. You have a power base and if you stand in it, you will achieve your goals. All conjunctions mark a new beginning. Open your heart to those who have love to give.

December 15: Jupiter trines Uranus
Are you ready to spread your wings? The motto for this aspect could be: something new, something different. Your world is opening up and you are likely to reach out for new experiences. Jupiter represents how you want to expand and improve your life and in Capricorn, your goals are under a magnifying glass. Keep alert to the opportunities around you. People help give you the lift-off you need.

December 19: Mars sextiles Saturn
Are you ready put the finishing touches on projects you have been working on for a while? As the action planet in powerful Scorpio contacts Saturn in ambitious Capricorn, your hard work over these last couple of months can pay off as you receive help from those in positions of authority. Let people know about your best ideas and the steps you have taken so far to implement them. If you have done your homework, you will get the help you need.

December 19: Mercury squares Neptune
As the communication planet travels through outspoken, enthusiastic Sagittarius, you are ready to tell everyone what you think. But watch that you don't say too much. Conversations can get muddy since Mercury encounters imaginative Neptune when trying to get to the truth is like hunting for a needle in a haystack. People have secrets now. You may have a few of your own. Have the conversations you want to have but realize that not everything will be revealed yet.

December 20: Venus enters Aquarius
Someone to love, someone who shares your values, someone you would choose as a friend as well as a partner. These are the desires of the love planet in egalitarian Aquarius. If you are already committed, this is a wonderful time to strengthen the bonds of friendship. If you are single and looking, this is a perfect transit to meet people by going to events and participating in activities that you enjoy. Venus will be in Aquarius until December 20.

December 21: Sun enters Capricorn
Set aside quiet time to reflect upon the past year and to count your blessings as the Sun enters earthy Capricorn and marks the Winter Solstice. Then, it's time to get a plan together and to prepare for the year ahead. What are your goals? What do you want to accomplish? The ambitiousness of this sign will help you make headway.

December 22: Mars sextiles Pluto
Are you ready to take your ideas and projects to the next level? Energetic Mars in Scorpio gives you determination and powerful Pluto in Capricorn gives the ambition to succeed along with people with clout and resources who are available to help. Keep your ears and eyes open so you can take advantage. Cooperative ventures are favored and business environments bring you face-to-face with people who can help you. If you know your chart, look to see what this pattern is activating so you can more fully take advantage of it.

December 24: Sun trines Uranus
This aspect gives you energy and courage. Now all you have to do is take advantage of it. Reach out to your support systems. If you have made recent adjustments and are willing to change your plans to make them better, people are willing to help you.

December 26: Solar eclipse in Capricorn
An eclipse in ambitious Capricorn lights up your connections with world. For many, your career represents your outer-world activities; for others, school or your volunteer work may characterize the core of your interactions. A solar eclipse, which occurs at the New Moon, marks an especially dramatic and important beginning. This is an achievement-oriented Moon, so see what mountain you want to climb and make a step-by-step plan to get there. Decide what you want to be responsible for and what you don't. Take action to make your life secure through tangible, practical accomplishments. As the Moon increases in light between the New and Full Moon, put energy into something you want to grow or become stronger or more important in your life.

December 27: Sun conjuncts Jupiter
Once a year, the Sun shines its light on optimistic Jupiter. This year this fortunate meeting occurs in Capricorn, the sign of ambition. The generosity of this planet is legendary. Jupiter expands the energy of the sign it occupies and brings helpers and angels.

December 28: Mercury enters Capricorn
It is time to be practical about what works or doesn't. Seek out those who have already accomplished what you hope to achieve. They can give you valuable advice and connect you with those who can help you. Your hard work now pays dividends down the road. Stay focused.

December 30: Mercury trines Uranus
Your ideas shine and sparkle in this harmonious contact with electric Uranus. Exciting conversations and stimulating conversations mark this period making this a great time to meet new people or to join groups that cause you to step out of old habits and thinking patterns.
