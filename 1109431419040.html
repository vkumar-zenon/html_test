On the Way to Work
by Jon L. Thomas, Ed.D.

College enrollment is down, and the costs of college education are on the rise. Many young people are opting out of other forms of post-secondary education and training, choosing instead to go directly to work. Whether through college or training, postsecondary education is vital to obtaining and growing a sustainable career and lifestyle. And without it, young people face an uncertain future.
