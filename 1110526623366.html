Theatre Northeast Children's Play: The Marvelous Machine

Students will perform  The Marvelous Machine   (The Inside Story of Our Insides)  by John R. Carrol. This is an engaging children's participation play that takes an imaginative trip through the human body.   With an ensemble of NTCC students playing multiple characters; a forgetful brain, a grumbling stomach, a corny, vaudevillian pair of lungs, the main stage of the Whatley will come alive with fun and facts about our amazing bodies.

Several weekday matinees will be performed for school children and then there will be two performances for the general public on Saturday, Dec. 7 at 7:30 p.m. and on Sunday, Dec. 8 at 2:05 p.m. General admission is $5, students, senior citizens and NTCC Faculty and Staff are $3, grade school children are $1 and NTCC students with ID are free. Reservations are not required to the weekend performances.
