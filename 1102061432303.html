2019/2020 PRIME INFIELD CLINIC

 
December, 2019
  
Prime Infield Clinic was designed as a progression program for infielders from the ages of 13 to 18 who are looking to take the next step in their infield development. This programs main philosophy is helping each player improve their base fundamentals as well as polishing their individual skills.
Some of the things that we will cover throughout this clinic include :
 
  • Footwork: for throws from every position and base. We will also be working on the footwork involved in playing first base.
  • Pre-Pitch Routine: working on having the best first step possible to the baseball.
  • Increase Arm Strength and Accuracy: we will work on the right way to throw the ball from each position and the different throws that each position has to make.
  • Arm Strength Development: using Dr. Jaeger and Dr. Jobe Exercises. We will be using their tube and light weight systems of pre-hab and arm strengthening.
  • Fundamentals: we will work on every play that you can come across in a game. Our goal is to turn the difficult play into a routine one; how to properly cover each base on throws from the outfield and from catchers.
