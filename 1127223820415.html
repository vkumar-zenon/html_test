My strategy for the holidays? Slow down .
We all have a continuous stream of energy during the day that is distracted by all the bright and shiny things vying for our attention. There is already natural beauty everywhere during the winter season, but as humans we just canât leave that alone, right? So we elevate it with lights, parties, music and food! This can add warmth and and a lot of zest to our lives, but it can also lead to burnout if we shine too bright. Are you starting to feel that?
If so, step up your home practice of meditation. Five to ten minutes of quiet sitting in the morning or evening will help you to bring a calmer mind to the mall, family gatherings and the buffet table.
The other option is to embrace the crazy, which can be really fun! Just remember to reconnect after, by keeping up your yoga practice. Even a few minutes a day can help to balance our physical and energetic bodies. Above all, enjoy yourself! There is a lot to love about this mad, mad world. Hope to see you soon and warmest wishes for a wonderful holiday season!
