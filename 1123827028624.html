Sweater Weather
Winter has arrived!

ï»¿Here's hoping that we don't receive much freezing rain, snow, and ice, but if we do, our goal will always be to provide service to all of our clients while keeping our teams safe in the field.

Pioneer's policy is to review each weather related event on a case by case basis with regard to the safety of our crews.  Inclement weather decisions for delays and closings are made by 8:00am each day and are communicated as follows: 

  • E-mail: Clients impacted by the inclement weather will receive an email and/or telephone alert. 
  • Facebook: Follow Pioneer on Facebook to stay up to date.
  • Website: Our website will be updated with the weather related message, which will be located in the bottom right corner of the home page via our Facebook page.
  • Office Voicemail: A message will be placed on our main office line (410-997-0936) if there is a closure or delay.

Should a wintry mix arrive, please help us continue service and keep our teams safe by doing the following:

  • Shovel sidewalks and driveways so your team has a clear route to your home.
  • Avoid slippery ice by salting sidewalks and path ways.
  • Contact Pioneer via the scheduling email address or text/call the office line alerting our staff to unsafe conditions, i.e. road closures, blocked driveways, cleaning changes, etc.
Holiday Tipping
Many clients ask whether tipping throughout the year is appropriate. We prefer that you please save your tips for an end of year gift. Holiday gifts are distributed to all team members on Christmas Eve, according to seniority. We have found this to be both fair and open.

Should you want to recognize your team and the service they have provided throughout the year, please provide your holiday/end of year recognition via check made out to Pioneer Custom Cleaning separate from your regularly scheduled cleaning.  Please note "End of Year Gift" in the memo line of your check so the office staff can be sure to direct it appropriately. Checks can also be mailed to our office. We kindly request all holiday bonuses by Friday, December 20th so we can have enough time to prepare them for delivery to each team member.
