The gift of the seasons
I love the change of seasons. Yes, winter could be one month shorter and the month of bugs and humidity of summer could be shifted to one more month of lovely fall weather, but I do look forward to each of the seasons.

Each season has itâs own powerful energy that impacts our thoughts and actions. We need only look at nature to see how we are encouraged to use the energy of each season. And we can feel it. Yes, we do need to keep moving our bodies and it feels amazing to get out into the crisp, cold air but winter is a time for deep reflection and deep rest. It is a time to replenish from the busy summer months.  

Make yourself a nice cup of tea, grab your journal and work on this exercise: 

Exercise: 
  1. Choose an area of your life that you find yourself feeling angst about. 
  2. Write down the specifics that are causing you concern. 
  3. Do deeper journaling on each of these specifics to gain insight and clarity.
  4. Tap on any charged emotions as you go through this.
