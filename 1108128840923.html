Artistic Director Mihae Lee's 10th Anniversary Season!
Dear Friends, 
 
Happy New Year from the Essex Winter Series! I hope you and your family are having a wonderful holiday season. For us, a new year means that our opening concert is just around the corner. I do hope you already have the dates in your 2020 calendar - January 12, February 16, March 8, and March 29 (and a very special surprise on April 26 - see below for more on that). I can't wait to share these exceptional concerts with you performed by our amazing roster of all-star artists!
 
Our opening concert on January 12th will be very personal for me, presenting my dear friend and one of the most esteemed violinists of our time, Ani Kavafian, plus eleven of our Fenton Brown Emerging Artist Alumni. We will be performing Copland's iconic " Appalachian Spring"  and Schubert's beloved "Trout" Quintet. I am so thrilled that so many of what I call "my kids" are excited to come back to perform for our audience again. This concert really epitomizes my vision for the EWS that I have been promoting since I became the Artistic Director ten years ago.  

Emerging Artists Alums are flutist Yoobin Son (2012); clarinetist Romie de Guise-Langlois (2013); bassoonist Adrian Morejon (2012); violinists Katie Hyun (2011 & 2018), Edson Scheid (2018), and Keiko Tokunaga (2015) ;violists Andy Lin (2011) and Luke Fleming (2015);  cellists Mihai Marica (2011) and Joann Whang (2017); and double bass player Joe Magar (2018). I am thrilled to tell you that ALL of them have successful careers and are making a difference in the music world. Please don't miss this unique concert that showcases the EWS's decade of commitment to nurturing music for the future!

If you haven't purchased tickets yet, you can easily do so from our  website  or by calling 860-272-4572. As with last season, Members at the Friend level and above are invited to join the musicians for a private reception after each concert. Other benefits of Membership are provided on the site along with wonderful photos from our past concerts and events. Please consider becoming a Member.

Be sure to "like" us on  Facebook as we frequently post stories and photos on our page, including news of our past emerging artists whose careers are continuing to thrive. If you have new neighbors who have recently moved into our area or friends who have not yet heard of our fabulous concerts, please be sure to tell them about EWS and encourage them to attend a concert with you. 
 
Finally, we are so grateful to all of you - our sponsors, advertisers, donors, and audience members. The wonderful EWS family is a vital part of our enriched community that we are so fortunate to live in.
 
I wish you and your family a happy and peaceful New Year, and I look forward to seeing you on January 12th!
 
Warmly,
Mihae
