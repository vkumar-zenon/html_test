Winners are still losing
In order to make it through the holidays without adding to their waistlines, several employees at the Dena'ina Wellness Center initiated a competition to help stay healthy and accountable. Participants have weekly weigh-ins, with prizes awarded every two weeks. Winners are determined by total percentage of body weight lost.

The week 2 winner was Melissa Bobby. The week 4 winner was Margaret Thomas. The week 6 winner was Margaret Thomas again!

Congratulations to the winners, and kudos to all the participants! All employees have free access to the Dena'ina Wellness Center gym and  group fitness classes. For more information, stop by the Activity Area desk or call 335-7588.
