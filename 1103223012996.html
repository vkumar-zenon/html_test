Holiday Schedule

Please note these changes to our regular worship schedule during the holidays:
  • There will be only one service this Sunday, Dec. 29 at 10 a.m. -- you won't want to miss it, as it is a special Christmas Festival of Lessons and Carols.
     
  • There will be no Wednesday service on New Year's Day, Jan. 1.
     
  • There will be no "Last Sunday Listening Session" in December.

We will be back to our regular schedule on Sunday, Jan. 5. We hope you had a merry Christmas and wish you the best in the new year!
