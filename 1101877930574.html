Take the Train to Lotus
  
SMART Train now stops in Larkspur and Downtown Novato. After SMART Train's ceremonial opening of its Larkspur extension on December 13, San Rafael is now connected to Larkspur by train.

This is great news for Indian food lovers who don't like traffic! Lotus Cuisine of India is literally across the road from the SMART Train Station and the San Rafael Transit Center. Lotus Curry House is just 2 minutes walk from the Downtown Station.
