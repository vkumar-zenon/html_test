Happy Thanksgiving, Student-Parents!
It's the time of year to gather with family and friends and give thanks for what we have. It's also the time of year that student-parents are juggling their time off school with their children's holiday breaks, all while studying for finals and finishing end-of-the-year projects at the same time! It can be overwhelming. The Parents & Families Program is here to help you however we can.

In this newsletter, you will find tips and tricks to make spring registration a breeze, meeting for student-parent support groups, and our last Fall semester dates for Hornet Family Study Care and Day Camp. Once the year is over, you can look forward to meeting other student-parents at our annual Student-Parent Spring Mixer!

As always, don't hesitate to reach out with questions or concerns. Good luck on finals!

Best,

Haley Myers Dillon
Director, Parents & Families Program
Load up your plate... and your Sac State Scheduler
Thanksgiving break is the perfect opportunity to get ahead and look at next semester's class offerings. You'll want to prepare for spring before you get bogged down with studying for finals. The Parents & Families Program recommends you look at the course offerings that before your registration date and come up with your ideal course schedule, a Plan B, and a Plan C. Use the student center's Sac State Course Scheduler to put your selected classes into a shopping cart and transfer it over to your course enrollment page before your registration date. That way you have everything ready to go!
