Dear Friends of Thirteen Strings,
 
Thirteen Strings is Ottawa's premier professional orchestra and is recognized across Canada for excellence in performances. We are very proud to celebrate our 43 rd season! However, we need your support of maintain the orchestra's high level of excellence.
 
Aldous Huxley wrote "After silence, that which comes nearest to expressing the inexpressible is music."
 
There are increasing costs to maintaining our level of excellence. Our ticket prices only cover a portion of our costs. In order to keep our concerts accessible to the entire community - from students to seniors on fixed incomes - our tickets our priced lower than the actual cost. Where does the balance come from? It comes from a mix of government, the corporate sector, fundraising events and people like you who generously contribute directly to Thirteen Strings. With your support, we will ensure that this vibrant orchestra remains a part of the Ottawa area musical landscapes in the years to come.
 
We know you are faced with tough choices as to where to target your own resources. With Thirteen Strings, you can be assured that the funds are used effectively to enable us to put on excellent and affordable concerts. Nearly 80 percent of the money we raise goes directly into producing those concerts.
 
We are also very proud and passionate about our educational and outreach projects. The Camerata Strings is a group of young players who join the orchestra as part of our mentorship program for one concert. Members from the orchestra also play at various senior residences in Ottawa. As well, we recorded in September our 2 nd CD for the international label NAXOS - a major achievement!
 
You are indeed one of the many people we count on in order to continue to deliver dazzling musical experiences, unique in Ottawa. Whether you are a season ticket subscriber or one of the many supporters of our fundraising events, your contribution helps make a difference.
 
Thank you for supporting Thirteen Strings! Please used the attached form to make a financial contribution in support of Thirteen Strings - Ottawa's own professional chamber orchestra.
 
Yours sincerely,
