In CDS Foundation news
The Dec. 13 deadline is approaching to nominate a CDS member for the annual Chicago Dental Society Foundation Vision Award.

The CDS Foundation Vision Award honors outstanding volunteerism and philanthropic commitment. Award recipients are selected for their contributions to oral health education and access to care for the underserved.

Nominees should exemplify professional integrity, honesty and a commitment to improving oral health. The award ceremony will take place during the Opening Session of the Chicago Dental Society Midwinter Meeting on Feb. 20.
