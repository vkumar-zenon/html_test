200hr Yoga Teacher Training + Apprenticeship  with  Lily Dougher and Margo Rosingana
JANUARY 2020
Directed by Lily Dougher and Margo Rosingana  
 
This training prepares you to teach inspired yoga classes as well as offers you the opportunity to explore the depth of your own being and awaken the innermost truth that resides within us all. This program is not intended to give you a sequence of postures and script to follow. This immersion into yoga will provide you with the essential and embodied knowledge of yoga asana and philosophy, while cultivating your own courage and creativity to plan and teach an experiential yoga practice.
