CYA Supports Friends of the Fairfax County Animal Shelter with our Weekly "Friends" Spotlight!
Hey Guys!
 
Clem and Felix are two older cats hanging out in Kitty City at the  Fairfax County Animal Shelter. These two sweet boys arrived to the Shelter with several other cats after their owner sadly passed away. Being older kitties, they are a bit out of sorts in a new environment, so they have been moved to Kitty City where they will have more room to move around and a quieter environment to get acclimated.

Clem (white spot on his face) and Felix (freckled nose) are quite fond of one another and are considered a bonded pair, which means they need to be adopted together (twice the love!). They love to cuddle up with each other, especially on these cold days! They are looking for a quiet home to call their own, could that be with you? 

Come see them at the  Fairfax County Animal Shelter  tomorrow when they open at noon!
 
  Fairfax County Animal Shelter  
Each year, nearly 5,000 pets, including cats, dogs, small mammals, reptiles and livestock, come to the Fairfax County Animal Shelter. The Shelter opens its doors-and its heart-to care for each and every pet. But the Shelter can't do it alone. Thankfully, Friends of the Fairfax County Animal Shelter is here to help. With donor and volunteer support, Friends funds important needs throughout the Shelter such as behavioral training and dental or medical care beyond what the shelter's budget can provide.   Together, we save lives!
