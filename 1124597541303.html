Self-Driving Car Accidents: Who's to Blame?
Self-driving cars have become more common in recent years as technology continues to progress.

The goal of self-driving cars is to assist drivers with basic tasks such as parallel parking, changing lanes, maintaining a safe speed, and looking out for negligent drivers on the road. 

Until automobile technology improves, however, these vehicles will likely cause accidents.
For more information on who's to blame in self-driving car accidents, check out our latest blog.
