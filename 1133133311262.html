Senior Goals for Law-Related Careers
(Click on bold words for links)

  • Start job hunting in January with a non-CSPD resume that's competitive in the current business market
  • Invest in Business Cards (VistaPrint) - this is your time to network at all functions
  • Start vetting companies you would like to work for and a title that would fit you (exp. Compliance Officer at SAP)
  • Open job search accounts (Indeed, Glassdoor, LinkedIn, Google for Jobs, Robert Half, USAjobs.gov, Idealist, SNAG, etc.)
  • Apply for jobs or social justice initiatives if you plan on taking a leap year or two.
  • Job Search Words: Compliance, Operations, Human Resources, and Management Analyst.

  • Work with Pre-Law Advisor on law school decisions (housing, financial aid, location, etc).

  • Apply for jobs or social justice initiatives if you plan on taking a leap year or two.
