Recipe Pairing for the Month

We paired our Clover Hill with Steamed Mussels

Wash well 4-6 pounds mussels
Place them in a large pot and add:
1/2 cup dry wine
1/2 cup minced fresh parsley and/or basil
2 tablespoons chopped garlic
Cover the pot, place it over high heat, and cook, shaking the pot occasionally, until most of the mussels are opened, about 10 minutes.
Remove the mussels to a serving bowl, strain the cooking liquid over them along with 1 tablespoon olive oil and the juice of one lemon.
Serve with crusty french bread.
