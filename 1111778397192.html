About Pam

A ccredited by Justin Stone in November 1984, Pam Towne has been teaching T'ai Chi Chih ever since. A teacher at heart, she has a Lifetime Secondary Teaching Credential in California.  Pam  has been a T'ai Chi Chh Teacher Trainer since 2001, leading Retreats, Intensive and Teacher Accreditation courses around the globe.  She also leads Seijaku (the advanced T'ai Chi Chih form) classes, retreats and Seijaku Teacher Accreditations.
