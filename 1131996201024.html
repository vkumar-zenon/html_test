Christmas Performance
**Please Read!!
We can't wait to see all the dancers perform in our Christmas Show, December 14th at Patterson Park in Murfreesboro.

No costumes required for the Christmas performance, children are encouraged to wear festive holiday outfits with red, green and white! Please make sure you have your appropriate shoes for your dances (tap, ballet, sneakers, etc.) We will have all the dancers warmup and lineup in the green room, where an instructor will bring them backstage. Parents are welcome to stay in the audience and enjoy the show! If your child is in SpotLite 1, 1/2, or 2, please pick them up on the side of the auditorium directly after their dance is finished. We will have them exit the stage and be waiting with an instructor until you pick them up. All other dancers will return to the green room until their next dance.

Tickets will be distributed Tuesday and Thursday this week. Please stop by either of those days between 4-7 to pick up your tickets.
