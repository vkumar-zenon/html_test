NAMI Metro General Meeting
Tuesday, November 19, 2019 at 7:00 p.m.  
      
 Michigan Rehabilitative Services  

Due to Thanksgiving week falling during our fourth Tuesday meeting we have moved the General meeting to the Third Tuesday.  
 
Speaker will be Vicki L. Wright, MA, LPC, CRC, CBSP Site Manager - Michigan Rehabilitation Services.
 
Michigan Rehabilitation Services is a statewide agency that provides vocational rehabilitation services to individuals who have employment barriers related to his/her disability as well as serving businesses who would like to have training regarding disability awareness, accessibility, accommodation strategies, and talent acquisition.  
 
This presentation will provide you with information regarding there general process and will also respond to your questions regarding such

CAR DONATIONS
TO SUPPORT NAMI Metro
 
Do you have an old vehicle just sitting and you want to dispose of it easily while getting a tax deduction and at the same time helping NAMI Metro?  Go to namimetro.org and on the top of the page click on the DONATE your car button.  You will be directed to the GLR Advanced Recycling web site where you will get all the information needed.  At the GLR site you will simply complete the required on-line information and GLR will make arrangements to pick it up.  GLR will donate a portion of the recycle value directly to NAMI Metro.  Lets CRUSH the Stigma...
