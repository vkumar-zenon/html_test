Santa Visits the Aviation Museum
Santa ditched his reindeer for a day, and got a ride from the red, white and blue 1947 NaVion. This airplane usually takes up kids for their first Young Eagle flight on First Sunday's at the Aviation Museum. Santa visited with over 100 young visitors, took pictures and listened to Christmas wish lists. Parents of these children brought gifts that were left under the Museum tree, that were then donated to the Rotary Club of Santa Paula, which distributed them to local families. It was a magical day for all involved!

What is SP-ACE?

SP-ACE or Santa Paula Aviation Career Explorers is a group of youth that meets every-other Friday, 6:45-9:00 PM. It's are a hands-on program open to young men and women from the 6th grade to 20 years old with an interest in learning more about careers in the field of Aviation. Do you know someone that would like to check out a meeting? Contact us!
