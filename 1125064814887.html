SHORELINE BALLET SPRING PERFORMANCE

Announcing our Spring Ballet!
Every year we celebrate our students achievements with a full length storybook ballet.  We are thrilled to announce that this year's ballet is, The Adventures of Alice in Wonderland. 
The performance will be Saturday, May 23rd 7:00pm and Sunday, May 24th 2:00pm at the Morgan School in Clinton. 
With original choreography, sets and costumes by the Shoreline Ballet artistic faculty we look forward to presenting this full length production to celebrate the end of our 2019-2020 season. 
All registered students from ages 3-18 will represent the many characters throughout the ballet.  The production will showcase the variety of dance forms that we offer such as Tap, Musical Theatre, Modern, Contemporary and Ballet.
Save the Date: Students in Levels 4-7 are encouraged to audition for leading roles  Saturday, January 25th 11:30-1:30pm
