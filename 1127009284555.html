New Survey: Older Adults Needs Assessment

The El Dorado County Health and Human Services Agency, Area Agency on Aging, in partnership with the Commission on Aging, is conducting a needs assessment survey of older adults in the community. This survey will help identify needs and challenges faced by current and future seniors. The survey results will also help the Area Agency on Aging in the planning process for senior services in El Dorado County that are part of the Area Plan submitted to the California Department of Aging.
Surveys can be completed on-line at

www.surveymonkey.com/r/2020OlderAdultSurvey

Copies are also available at the Senior Centers in Placerville, El Dorado Hills and South Lake Tahoe. If you would like a survey mailed to you, please contact Yasmin Hichborn with the El Dorado County Health & Human Services Agency by phone at 530-642-4833 or via email at yasmin.hichborn@edcgov.us  or contact the Commission on Aging at (530) 642-7276.
