HAPPY HOLIDAYS!


Dear NCPG Members, 

As our calendar year comes to an end, I would like to thank each and every one of you for your tremendous participation and involvement in NCPG, the overwhelming support and encouragement you have provided your peers and our community, and the valuable feedback you have given our amazing teachers and hard working Board! We are already hard at work planning an exciting schedule of events and activities in 2020! 

Also, special thanks to our Special Events Directors, Kristina Spellman and Candice Norman, for planning such an amazing All Class Santa Playdate last week at the Children's Museum of Phoenix! We truly appreciate your time and efforts in planning such a fun event for the whole family. It was the first time my own children met Santa without tears - so thank you for that! :)

In January, recruitment for available 2020-2021 Board positions will be in full swing, so if you are interested in learning more - please plan to join us for our "Board & Bites" informational session at the start of our January 6, 2020 Board Meeting. See details below.

A few final reminders before winter break: 
  • Salvation Army Christmas Angels - ***Toy Collection is this Wednesday!*** Bring your donations to drop off with our Community Outreach Directors on the sidewalk fronting Hutton Hall between 8:30-9:45am. Please see complete instructions below. Thank you to everyone for your amazing generosity! Your overwhelming support and donations are truly heartwarming!

  • Fall Education Survey - Thank you to everyone who took a moment to provide feedback and completed our Fall Survey! We greatly appreciate and value your input. 
  • Class Dates - REMINDER: this Wednesday, December 11th is our last class before winter break! Our first class of next year will be on Wednesday, January 8, 2020.
  • Board Recruitment - Please join us at the beginning of our Monday, January 6, 2020 Board meeting for our "Board and Bites" informational session at 6:30pm to learn more about available roles. Board meetings are held at Christ Church School (in the Parish Hall) on Lincoln Drive in Paradise Valley. Please email president@ncpgaz.org and vp@ncpgaz.org to let us know if you plan to attend and/or if you have any questions.

I hope to see everyone on Wednesday for our final class of 2019! Wishing you and your families a very happy and healthy holiday season! You can email me at  president@ncpgaz.org if you have any questions or find me on Wednesday morning to chat!

xo,

ALL SAINTS' EPISCOPAL DAY SCHOOL

The mission of All Saints' Episcopal Day School is to educate children in the light of God - mind, body, and soul - in a nurturing community. We combine academic excellence with spiritual and more formation, preparing our students to lead fruitful lives and serve a world in need.

Since 1963, All Saints' has provided a nurturing academic, social, and spiritual community where children are encouraged to learn, work, and play together with respect, responsibility, and understanding.

Today, we continue this tradition by developing thoughtful, confident, life-long learners, inspired and well-equipped to contribute to a dynamic and ever-changing world. The best way to know if All Saints' is the right fit for your child is through our Open House on January 22, 2020. Learn more and RSVP today at www.allsaints.org. Follow us on Facebook (@PhoenixASEDS) and Instagram (aseds_phoenix).


ARIZONA TUITION CONNECTION
Send your child to the school you always dreamed of.
Almost from the day our children are born we dream of sending them to a school that will provide the education and social experiences to guide them to a successful life.
For some, those schools are religious schools.  For others, it is art, science, or academics that are important.  And for some still, there are special needs to consider.  Often, those schools we dream of are private schools involving significant tuition expenses.  Arizona Tuition Connection guides parents through the process of using private school tax credits to pay tuition expenses for students attending K-12 private schools.
  • Scholarships are available to families of all income levels.
  • The best time to start this process is while your child is in pre-K, but we welcome conversations with parents of kids of all ages.  
  • It is possible to have 100% of your tuition expenses covered.
Join us to find out more. Information, including a list of coaching sessions, can be found at  www.arizonatuitionconnection.com.
