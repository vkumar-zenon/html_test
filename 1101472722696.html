WHAT DOES VISION & BRAIN TRAINING DO?

IMPROVEMENTS IN ATHLETICS ...
  • Ability to see and react faster to passes and shots. 
  • Greater ability to see plays developing. Recognize, distinguish and track more players on the ice, field or court. 
  • Create higher save percentage, higher batting average, stronger forehand and backhand reaction time (tennis), effective puck handling and dribbling, quicker reaction to rebounds. 
  • Maintain focus throughout an entire game or match. 
  • Maintain focus at the end of a game/match, or in a high stress situation like the last few minutes, overtime, or the championship game. 
  • IMPROVED PUCK TRACKING
  • Peripheral Awareness: is the ability to see actions or objects that are not in your direct line of vision, but instead take place on the sides of your vision.  By improving a goalies peripheral awareness they will be able to read plays more precisely while maintaining visual contact with the puck.
  • Cognitive Processing: is the ability to take in and use any information like sights and sounds. The quicker a goalie is able to read and react to a play the quicker and more precisely they to get into position.
  • Visual Reaction Time: is the time required to respond to visual stimuli. The quicker a goalie is able to pick up the puck, the hand of the opponents stick, the location of the stick etc. the quicker they will react. 
IMPROVEMENTS IN ACADEMICS ...

Have A Professional Goalie Coach Attend Your Team Practice


McGuire Goaltending offers a number of different services for Team Goaltender Training. During the past season we supplied goalie coaches and mentors to over 50 teams in the GHTL and OMHA.

Goaltenders repeat bad habits during team practices when they are not being watched by a professional goalie coach. It is just too hard for the team coach and assistants to focus on the development of the entire team and also help the goaltenders.

Our coaches come to your practice and will ensure that your teams goalies are constantly improving during the season.

With our help your team can create its own customized training program for developing your goaltenders.

Goalie Coach Attending Team Practice

Have a goalie coach attend your team practice and work with your goalies. We would require Blue Line In for at least 30 - 45 minutes and then you can incorporate your goalies into the rest of the practice. Ideally we would use the entire practice time with the goaltenders.

Most team's who use this service will have a goalie coach attend their practice at least once per week. A package of 20 sessions will usually cover an entire season.
