ALG Employee Health and Benefits
From the desk of
Lynda Knight

As we head into 2020, there are three new changes to our ALG 401k Plan.

The information below will provide you with details on these three areas of change:

  • 401k Hardship Withdrawal Changes

  • How you can request fund reports be sent to you.

  • In-Plan Roth Transfers

Please see the below information for more details.
401(k) Hardship Withdrawal IRS Regulation Changes for 2020

Effective 1/1/2020, there will be several IRS regulation changes for 401(k) Plan hardship withdrawals:

  • The current 6 month suspension following a hardship withdrawal will be eliminated.

  • If you are currently suspended from the 401(k) plan due to a hardship withdrawal, your suspension will end on 1/1/2020 and your 401(k) deferrals will automatically resume at the rate prior to your hardship withdrawal.

  • Earnings will be included in your available balance for hardship withdrawals.

  • You will not be required to take a loan prior to a hardship withdrawal.

If you have any questions, please contact Murfee Meadows, Inc. at 205.871.9515 or 800.600.0947.
Internet Availability of Fund Reports

Beginning on January 1, 2021, as permitted by regulations adopted by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission, paper copies of fund shareholder reports will no longer be sent by mail, unless you specifically request to receive paper copies of the reports. Instead, the reports will be made available on a website, and you will be notified by mail each time a report is posted. You will be provided with a website link to access that report.

If you have already elected to receive shareholder reports electronically, you will not be affected by this change and you need not take any action.

You may elect to receive all future reports in paper, free of charge. You can request to continue receiving paper copies of your fund reports by calling 1-800-283-3427. Your election to receive reports in paper will apply to all the funds in which you invest. If you have already elected to receive Regulatory Documents via electronic delivery or have already provided a response to a previous notice, you do not need to respond again.
In-Plan Roth Transfers: Ability to convert 401(k) to ROTH 401(k)

In-Plan Roth Transfers are now permitted as of January 1st, 2020. This will allow any ALG 401(k) participant to transfer the non-Roth portion of their 401(k) into a designated Roth account within the same plan.

This does require you to pay taxes on the 401(k) funds you choose to move, as a Roth 401k account is taxed at the point of contribution, allowing for a tax-free retirement.

For more details on this conversion process and your specific needs, please reach out to Mary Rob with Murfee Meadows at 205.871.9515.

Safety & Facility News
Be aware of your surroundings!

As we head into 2020, safety on the plant floor, in the ADC, loading docks and even in the office areas, continues to be an important step that EVERYONE needs to follow!

Here are just a few tips that can serve as reminders to keep everyone safe and accident-free for the new year.
  • Keep every area clean, organized and clutter-free!

  • Avoid someone taking a nasty fall by immediately cleaning up any spills, especially ice around the ice machine. Pay special attention and remove, anything that is left in an office floor or plant aisle that someone could trip over.

  • Use equipment properly. No short cuts, use only the proper steps for operation. Do not put yourself or someone else in danger!

As a safety goal to keep in mind, April 30th, 2020 will mark 12 months of NO ACCIDENTS at ALG. Let's stay alert and aware so that ALG is injury-free in 2020!

HOUSEKEEPING NOTES

As we all like to enjoy a clean break room area, please make sure you are following the these steps:

  1. Clean up behind yourself after a meal or snack. Clean wipes are proved for this very reason and no one else should have to do this!
  2. Do not leave any dirty dishes in the sink! If you are not going to pick them up until when you leave for the day, go ahead and wash them, and leave them in the dish drainer.
  3. Only rinse dirty dishes on the side with the garbage disposal, allowing for any remaining food to be washed down into disposal. Remember to leave the water running and flip on the power to the disposal to complete this process. Don't leave food in sink or in disposal.

Following these steps will keep everything clean for us all to enjoy.
