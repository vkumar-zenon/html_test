The First Annual Greater Fort Myers Chamber of Commerce Cornhole & Chili Competition takes place on Saturday, January 25 from noon to 3:00PM at Hammond Stadium at Centurylink Sports Complex.

The Cornhole Competition is open to 32 teams, with single elimination and consolation rounds. Individual teams are $200 (increases to $300 after 12/31/19) , and include 2 players, 2 alternates, and 4 Festival of Beer tickets. Check-in for the Cornhole Competition is at 11:00AM. Corporate teams are $500 and include 2 players, 2 alternates, 4 Festival of Beer tickets, 1 lawn sign with logo, and 4 t-shirts.

The Chili Cook-Off will have 5 teams, with a minimum of 2 people and maximum of 4 people per team. Corporate teams are $500 and include tickets to the Festival of Beers, 1 lawn sign with logo, and 4 t-shirts. Individual teams are $200 and include Festival of Beers tickets. Contact Debra DeHaven for more important details about the Chili Cook-off. Chili teams begin cooking at 8:00AM.

This event is presented in collaboration with the Fort Myers Mighty Mussels , and participants and attendees of the Cornhole & Chili Competition will receive tickets for the 15th Annual Ballpark Festival of Beers, which runs from 2:00-6:00PM on the same afternoon in the stadium.

A $30 spectator ticket is available and includes the chili tasting, cornhole tournament, and Festival of Beers ticket.
