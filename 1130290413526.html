Fall is upon us...

With fall just beginning, the cooler weather and winter months will be here before we know it. For most, fall is the time of year to enjoy relief from the heat, watch the changing leaves and prepare for the holiday season ahead. It is important to perform regular home maintenance to ensure that your home is at its best to prevent potentially costly damage and is operating as efficiently as possible.
 
Below are fall preventative home maintenance tips that every homeowner should follow.  

 
  1. Check for drafts in windows and doors.
  2. Remove screens and install storm windows
  3. Seal gaps where critters could enter.
  4. Test smoke detectors and CO batteries if necessary.
  5. Change Indoor Air Filters.
  6. Locate all outdoor water valve and shut-off to prevent pipes freezing.
  7. Inspect exterior trim, gutters and downspouts.
  8. Change indoor air filters.
  9. Clean and adjust ceiling fans (so that air circulates upward).
  10. Check railings on decks and porches for support.
