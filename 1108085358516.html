Haystack Holidays
Holiday Fun At The Beach!
 
The Fireside Social Orchestra

The winter holiday season is in full swing in Cannon Beach, and there's something fun going on every week from now thru the New Year; come join us at the beach and make the small town holiday memories you'll cherish forever! 

Be sure to check the Haystack Holidays events list to find out what's happening during your visit, from wreath making classes and cookie tastings, to the Library's annual Holiday Tea party and our cherished Lighting of the Lamp, you'll have plenty of chances to meet Santa, carol in the fresh coastal air and cruise the town's decorations as a guest judge. 

Holiday Foods Around the World returns this year from  Thursday, Dec. 26 - Saturday, Dec. 28 when  participating Cannon Beach restaurants feature unique food and beverage specials highlighting many different countries and cultures. These mashups of foreign influences and local chef culture result in delicious and exciting results.. come see what your favorite chefs are dreaming up!

Christmas week visitors are in for a treat at the Haystack Holidays Winter Concert -  "A Fireside Christmas" performed by The Fireside Social Orchestra. They specialize in the music of the Nineteenth Century on period instruments and voices. This memorable evening of heritage Christmas Music is at the intimate Coaster Theatre Playhouse on  Thursday, December 26, 2019. 
Tickets and more information are here. Learn more about the Fireside Social Orchestra, their instruments and songs here.

As always, little events pop up at the last minute! Be sure to keep an eye on our Facebook page before and during your visit to see what's going on! 
Planning Your 2020 Vacation? 

As your clan gathers this holiday season, or at least checks in over the phone, it's a great time to plan your 2020 activities and family vacations. Keep these Cannon Beach classics in mind, and come see us sometime this year! 

Savor Cannon Beach  March 12-15, 2020. Four days of wine tastings, culinary events and a wine walk showcasing Northwest wineries in Cannon Beach, Oregon. Fantastic food!

Get Lit at the Beach-A Gathering for Readers April 3-5, 2020. A literary festival bringing together authors and readers to celebrate a mutual love of books and reading.

Fat Bike Festival  April 24-26, 2020. Enjoy a weekend of fun fat bike events while exploring the beaches and lovely community of Cannon Beach by bike. Organized rides, games & contests.

Spring Unveiling Arts Festival  May 1-3, 2020. With visiting and local artists providing unveilings of their latest works, demonstrations and presentations in a town known for its vibrant art scene, this festival is a great way for visitors to experience the arts in Cannon Beach.

North Coast Culinary Fest  May 15-17, 2020. Experience the ultimate in coastal culinary arts, in honor of James Beard! This growing festival offers beverage pairing workshops, hands on cooking opportunities and virtually unlimited dining delights!

Sandcastle Contest  June 13, 2020 the oldest competition of its kind in the Pacific Northwest and the most popular event in Cannon Beach. Dozens of teams of professional sand sculpture artists, amateur groups and families will construct remarkable creations in the sand during the event. Make a weekend out of it and start your summer off in the sand!

Florentine Chocolate Cookie Sandwich

From Bob & Lenore's Cookbook 
EVOO Cannon Beach 
188 S. Hemlock
503.436.8555
evoo.biz

'Tis the season for visiting friends, bake sales and cookie exchanges, so it seemed timely to find the fanciest cookies in the EVOO Cookbook that you can also easily make at home! 
Delicate, lacy Florentines can be enhanced with your favorite nuts or coconut (why not both!) and are filled with a slather of melted chocolate ... add these to your cookie plate and get ready for the applause!
 
Ingredients:
  • Â¼ pound butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch salt
  • 1 cup quick cooking oats
  • 1 cup almonds, hazelnuts, coconut, or combination of these
  • Chocolate ganache, as needed (see recipe) or simply melt good chocolate
Method:

Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF.  Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Cream the butter and sugar together with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth; add the egg and beat until incorporated; add the vanilla, baking soda and salt and beat until smooth.
Meanwhile, in a food processor, combine the oats and nuts and pulse several times to grind. Add this to the creamed mixture and beat until incorporated. The dough will be sticky. Drop by the teaspoonful, about 3 inches apart, onto the prepared pan; wet your finger tips or back of spoon to flatten the dough slightly.

Bake for 6 minutes until lightly golden. Remove from the oven and cool completely on wire racks. Place 1 -2 teaspoons ganache in the center of 1 cookie; place another cookie on top and press gently to make a sandwich; hold for service. Cookies will set eventually at room temperature about 1 hour.
