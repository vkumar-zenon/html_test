International Parent Community

When:   Friday, January 10th 2020.
10:00 - 11:30 am
Where:   International House Davis, Activities Room (downstairs)
Other:  Free and open to the community, all are welcome! 

ï»¿ The Davis International Parent Community helps families from overseas form bonds and feel more comfortable in Davis. International parents get together once a week at I-House to talk about American culture, the school system, life in Davis, after-school activities and other information useful for parents of school-age children.

The International Parent Community is for:
New parents of K-12 students who want to meet others and learn about life in Davis,
Long-time international residents with helpful advice for newcomers,
And, local parents eager to share information and meet their neighbors from around the world.
