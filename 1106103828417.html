It's that time of year:
The annual all-member 
Holiday Sale & Show!

If you've been around the Kittery Art Association for a while, you know this to be true:  this is a BIG show!  For those newer to the KAA, we stock the gallery with a lot of our works of art that make fabulous unique, handmade, local gifts.

We hang art on the wall, and we set up tables and pedestals and other festive displays to show off our greeting cards, pottery, knit goods, scarves, mittens, ornaments, jewelry, stationery, journals, hats, prints, calendars, baskets, felted critters and whatever additional wonders our members have created.

In addition to regular Thursday 2-5 and Sat/Sun 11-5 hours, the gallery will be open on Fridays from 2-5 during the show.  Opening Day is Thursday December 5 and the last day is Sunday, December 15.  Just two short weeks for all of this awesomeness.

Holiday Concerts

That's right, there are TWO.  Susie Burke, David Surette and Kent Allyn have been so popular in the past that they have graciously agreed to perform TWO concerts for the KAA, on December 13 and 14 at the First Congregational Church of Kittery Point.

You can buy tickets online via our website, or by visiting the gallery, or by calling the gallery to reserve with your credit card.  Lastly, there will be some tickets available at the door for $20 on the night of each show.  Both shows start at 7pm.

NEW for the 2020 Artist Membership:  
Become part of the Kittery Art Association Member Virtual Galleries! 

All KAA members are welcome to have a virtual gallery page for a fee of $10 per calendar year, usually paid with your annual membership, but can be added to your membership anytime.  This is a great way to share information about you as an artist and the works you create.  

To see an example and find out how you can do this, visit the KAA Website and under the Membership menu click KAA Member Virtual Galleries.  All questions can be sent to artistpages@kitteryartassociation.org

Life Figure Drawing at the KAA
Mondays, 10 am to 1 pm 
drawing by Lance Hellman
Join us on Mondays at the KAA for our Figure Drawing sessions.  Practice your work with a live model holding a variety of poses over the course of the session.  Please note there will be no session on Monday, December 9 due to the Holiday Show.

Cost remains at an affordable $10.

As always, please bring your choice of medium.  Bring an easel if you need one.  Chairs and drop cloths are provided.  The session takes place at the Kittery Art Association, 8 Coleman Avenue, Kittery Point. 

For more info, please email us at info@kitteryartassociation.org or give us a ring at (207) 451-9384.
